## Notes

- Ingolstadt's open-data catalog (ingolstadt.de/opendata, mirrored at ingolstadt.bydata.de) lists only 6 datasets. The only shapefile is "Grenzen und Bezeichnungen der Stadt- und Unterbezirke" (https://www.ingolstadt.de/shape-Stadtbezirke-Unterbezirke) — this is the ~23 Stadtteile/Unterbezirke layer, NOT WBZ. Verified explicitly: catalog has no Wahl-/Stimmbezirk dataset.
- Bayern federated portal (open.bydata.de) returns Munich's WBZ dataset for the keyword search but no Ingolstadt counterpart; bydata is only an aggregator of city portals so this confirms Ingolstadt has not published any WBZ shapefile.
- Geoportal `stadtplan.ingolstadt.de` is a public web map — it does not expose downloadable WBZ polygons or a documented WMS/WFS endpoint for election layers.
- BTW result pages (`bundestagswahl2021.bayern.de`, `btwahl.ingolstadt.de`, `bundestagswahl2025.bayern.de/ergebnis/grafik/215.html`) provide only Wahlkreis-216/215 (constituency) summaries, not municipal WBZ.
- The city-level "Amt fuer Verkehrsmanagement und Geoinformation" (vmg@ingolstadt.de) explicitly lists "Kommunale Karten und Geodaten; Erhalt" as a service — this is the correct first contact for WBZ polygons. The state surveying office (ADBV / Amt fuer Digitalisierung, Breitband und Vermessung Ingolstadt, poststelle@va-in.bayern.de) is NOT relevant — it handles Liegenschaftskataster, not municipal election geometries.
- Stadtrat 2026 results page uses 4-digit Stimmbezirk IDs (0212/0213/0214 visible) within Stadtbezirk groupings (Mitte, Nordwest, Suedost). For a city of ~140k inhabitants with ~100k eligible voters, expected WBZ count is ~80-120 (in-person) plus separate Briefwahlbezirke. The tabular data exists internally; only the polygons are unpublished.
- Recommended outreach order: (1) vmg@ingolstadt.de cc open.data@ingolstadt.de requesting WBZ shapefiles for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025; (2) if no response, briefwahl@ingolstadt.de (Wahlamt) which holds the authoritative Wahlbezirkseinteilung. CC the Wahlamt phone 0841 305-1715 contact if email lapses.
