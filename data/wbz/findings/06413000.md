## Notes

- No public Wahlbezirk shapefile / GeoJSON for any year (2013, 2017, 2021, 2025) is published online. Searches covered: opendata.hessen.de (region tag offenbach-am-main-stadt — only non-geo datasets), govdata.de (zero hits for "Offenbach Wahlbezirk"), geoportal.hessen.de spatial-objects index (no Offenbach WBZ), Geoportal Regionalverband FrankfurtRheinMain (no city-level WBZ), and offenbach.de (Wahlen, Statistik, Vermessung sections).
- The city's geoportal at `http://geoportal.offenbach.de/mapbender/application/geoportal_offenbach` is intranet-only (cert error / 404 from outside; explicitly noted as intranet-only in council document 2021-26/DS-I(A)0280, June 2022).
- A 2025 CCC media talk ("Ein Open Data Portal kommt nicht in die Gaenge") uses Offenbach as a case study of a stalled municipal open-data rollout — confirms there is still no published portal as of 2025/2026.
- offenbach.de publishes only PDFs: a `Strassenverzeichnis` PDF (street -> Wahlbezirk lookup table, not geometry) at `/medien/bindata/of/Statistik_und_wahlen_/wahlen/VERZ-Strassenverzeichnis-fuer-das-Internet.pdf`, and two general subdivision Stadtplaene (Stadtteile = 21 districts; Statistische Bezirke). Neither is WBZ polygon data.
- Offenbach has ~133k inhabitants; expected WBZ count is roughly 60-90 polygons. Definitely not the 21 Stadtteile or the smaller Statistische-Bezirke set.
- Recommended request path: vermessungsamt@offenbach.de (geo primary), cc wahlen@offenbach.de. Ask explicitly for shapefile / GeoJSON of Urnenwahlbezirke for BTW 2013, 2017, 2021, 2025 (boundaries may have changed across cycles). No open-data broker contact available — if Vermessungsamt is unresponsive, escalate via Buergerbuero or via the Magistrat / IT-Stabsstelle referenced in PIO motion DS-I(A)0280.
- Hessian state-level geoportals (HVBG, geoportal.hessen.de) host municipality boundaries and INSPIRE layers but do NOT publish municipal Wahlbezirke for Offenbach; no realistic upstream alternative.
