## Notes

- Munich publishes Stimmbezirke + Briefwahlbezirke shapefiles for every election since 2017 (BTW 2017, BTW 2021, LTW 2018, LTW 2023, EUW 2019, EUW 2024, KomW 2020, KomW 2026). All as zipped ESRI shapefiles under dl-by-de/2.0.
- BTW 2013 is NOT on the portal — coverage starts at 2017. Worth emailing wahlamt.kvr@muenchen.de or geoinfo.kom@muenchen.de to ask whether 2013 WBZ geometries are archived internally.
- BTW 2025: only the 4 Wahlkreise (federal constituency boundaries) are published, not the ~700+ WBZ. The 2026 Kommunalwahl Stimmbezirke ZIP is up; BTW 2025 Stimmbezirke may follow the same release pattern as past cycles but is missing as of this search. KomW 2026 boundaries may be very close to the BTW 2025 boundaries used the same year.
- Maintainer field on the dataset page lists "Kreisverwaltungsreferat, Wahlen und Abstimmungen" as the data steward, so wahlamt.kvr@muenchen.de is the correct first contact for both 2013 and 2025 WBZ.
- Projection/CRS and exact feature counts not shown on the metadata page; need to download a ZIP to confirm. Munich typically uses ETRS89/UTM zone 32N (EPSG:25832).
- Same dataset is mirrored on open.bydata.de (Bayern federated portal) and govdata.de — same files, same URLs.
