## Notes

- Verified via WFS GetCapabilities and resultType=hits queries against `wfs_skre_wahlbezirke`: 6 layers exposed (`skre_stimmbezirke` n=118, `skre_bundestagswahlbezirke` n=2, plus `skre_landtagswahlbezirke`, `skre_ratswahlbezirke`, `skre_kreiswahlbezirke`, `skre_europawahlbezirke`). 118 Stimmbezirke is consistent with WBZ-level granularity (~140 expected; not the 9 Stadtbezirke or 19 Stadtteile, which sit in a separate `wfs_skre_kommunalbezirke` service).
- Schema has no year/election attribute and metadata explicitly states "Die Daten enthalten die aktuell gültigen Wahlbezirke" — the published service is a single live snapshot (currently 2025-aligned), so 2013/2017/2021 polygons are not retrievable from the portal. The Open.NRW / govdata mirrors point at the same KRZN WFS, so they do not add historical coverage.
- Per BTW 2025: Krefeld covers Bundestagswahlkreise 110 (Krefeld I - Neuss II) and 111 (Krefeld II - Wesel II) - matches the 2 features in `skre_bundestagswahlbezirke`.
- Recommended ask of Krefeld (single email to wahlen@krefeld.de, cc gis@krefeld.de): historical Stimmbezirk shapefiles for BTW 2013, 2017, 2021. Wahlamt is the listed Ansprechpartner for the dataset; GIS team likely holds archived versions in their internal system. statistik@krefeld.de is a softer fallback if no response.
- Stadtarchiv Krefeld holds historical maps but is unlikely to have machine-readable shapefiles; do not prioritise.
- Geoportal Krefeld viewer: https://geoportal-niederrhein.de/krefeld/geoportal/ ; service hub: https://www.krefeld.de/de/geoportal
