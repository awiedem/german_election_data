## Notes

- Salzgitter has ~100 Stimmbezirke per public references (city note: "100 electoral districts can be viewed with a connection to Google Maps"), distinct from the 31 Stadtteile and 7 Stadtbezirke. The Google-Maps embed is illustrative only — no shapefile / GeoJSON download is exposed.
- govdata.de search for "Salzgitter" returns 1 spatial dataset only: Flächennutzungsplan (GML, dl-de/by-2-0, last updated 14.04.2026). No Wahlbezirk dataset.
- Niedersachsen Geoportal (geoportal.geodaten.niedersachsen.de) — keyword "Wahlbezirke" returned no Salzgitter record (search endpoint returned 503 during this run, but harvested NI catalogue for other municipalities like Göttingen/Wolfsburg/Braunschweig is the same channel; Salzgitter has not been harvested there as of this check). NI guidance: "electoral boundary data management is handled by individual municipalities."
- SZMaps replaced the older Geoportal/Stadtplan in 2022 (built with TOPO graphics). Themes: Stadtleben, Bauen, Umwelt, Tourismus & Freizeit. No Wahlen theme.
- FragDenStaat has no granted Salzgitter Wahlbezirk-geodata request on file (only general Stadt-Salzgitter authority pages).
- Recommended outreach order: (1) gerard.jaschkowitz@stadt.salzgitter.de — Geoinformation Betriebsleiter, most likely to hold/produce shapefiles; (2) cc wahlbuero@stadt.salzgitter.de to confirm boundary editions used for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 (boundaries change between elections); (3) LGLN Katasteramt Salzgitter only as fallback if city does not respond. Ask explicitly for BTW-specific boundaries (2013, 2017, 2021, 2025) and confirm the unit is Urnenwahlbezirk (~100 polygons), not Stadtbezirk (7) or Stadtteil (31).
