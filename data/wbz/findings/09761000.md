## Notes

- Augsburg has ~210 Urnenwahlbezirke for Bundestagswahlen (city-internal numbering 101-722 across 17 Stadtbezirke). The Geodatenamt's address-lookup app (https://augsburg.maps.arcgis.com/apps/instant/lookup/...) reveals per-address Wahlbezirk assignment, indicating polygons exist internally on ArcGIS — they are simply not published as a public layer.
- The ArcGIS portal (augsburg.maps.arcgis.com) was searched: visible items cover 3D-Stadtmodell, Solarflächenkataster, Adressauskunft, school districts, planning areas. No public Wahlbezirk feature service was found.
- For 2013/2017/2021/2025: best path is direct email to Geodatenamt (service.geodatenamt@augsburg.de) cc'ing Wahlamt (wahlen@augsburg.de) requesting historic WBZ polygon shapefiles. The Geodatenamt is the technical custodian; the Wahlamt sets the boundaries.
- Note that "Stadtbezirke" (17) and "Stadtteile/Planungsräume" (42) are distinct from Wahlbezirke and are the geometries most likely to surface in casual portal searches — care needed not to confuse them.
- Augsburg has not published any historic election shapefiles as open data. The Open-Data office is new (est. Feb 2024) and election geodata is not yet in their 180+ dataset catalog.
- Bavaria-wide alternative: the Bayerisches LDBV publishes Wahlkreis (federal constituency) boundaries but NOT WBZ; municipalities own WBZ geometries.
