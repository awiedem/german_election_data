## Notes

- WFS GetCapabilities at `https://geoportal.leverkusen.de/qgis/wms/Administrative_Gebietseinheiten/Admin_Gebietseinheiten_extern` returns exactly 3 FeatureTypes: Stadtgrenze, Stadtbezirke, Stadtteilgrenzen. No Wahl/Stimm layer.
- City confirms 26 Wahlbezirke for council elections; Stimmbezirke (the BTW-relevant Urnenwahlbezirke) capped at 2,500 residents → expect roughly 100-130 SB polygons per BTW. Maps exist internally (embedded as figures in council PDFs) but are not published as vector data.
- For BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 WBZ polygons, direct request to gis@stadt.leverkusen.de (cc 330-wahlen@stadt.leverkusen.de) is required. Historical (2013/2017) boundaries may not be archived as vector data — expect partial response.
- Tabular per-Stimmbezirk results are publicly available at https://wahl.leverkusen.de (e.g. BTW 2025 path: `/vote/Bundestagswahl-2025-02-23/05316000/...`) but linked only to SB IDs, not geometry.
- Alternative routes checked, all negative for Leverkusen WBZ: govdata.de, opengeodata.nrw.de, open.nrw, GEOportal.NRW, offenedaten-koeln.de (Köln only).
- Postal address for both depts: Friedrich-Ebert-Platz 1, 51373 Leverkusen. Kataster also reachable via 62@stadt.leverkusen.de / Tel. 0214 406-6201.
