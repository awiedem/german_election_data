## Notes

- BTW administration: Stadt Amberg jointly runs BTW WK 231 (pre-2021: WK 232) with Landkreis Amberg-Sulzbach + Landkreis Neumarkt; result portal is wahlen.amberg-sulzbach.de. The city itself maintains its own Stimmbezirk IDs `093610000XXX`.
- WBZ count for the city of Amberg appears to be ~25–26 (consistent with prompt). The BTW2025 result tree exposes individual `ergebnisse_stimmbezirk_09361000XXXX.html` pages enumerating each WBZ but no geometry.
- The only public visual is the single PDF `Wahlbezirke_Karte.pdf` (~16 MB raster). It is undated in metadata but last-modified April 2023; comments from city staff in BayernPortal suggest WBZ have been stable across recent elections, so this map likely also documents the BTW2021 / BTW2025 layout. No similar archival map for BTW 2013 or 2017 was findable.
- Amberg's open-data presence (since late 2023 on open.bydata via Smart-City) currently publishes tabular election results only (Europawahl 2024); no polygon datasets.
- Recommended outreach: single email to martin.schafbauer@amberg.de (Wahlamt) cc michael.birner@amberg.de (Liegenschaftsamt), asking whether (a) the Wahlbezirk geometries underlying `Wahlbezirke_Karte.pdf` exist as SHP/GeoJSON and can be released under CC BY 4.0 / dl-de-zero-2-0 for academic use, and (b) archival WBZ geometries for BTW 2013/2017 are still on file. ADBV Amberg (state office) is unlikely to hold these — WBZ are drawn by the city Wahlamt, geometries usually held by Liegenschaftsamt or Tiefbauamt. Smart-City team (info via amberg.bydata.de or general Rathaus contact) is a tertiary route if a fresh dataset publication is feasible.
- No FragDenStaat IFG request for Amberg WBZ geodata is on record (search returned only generic templates).
