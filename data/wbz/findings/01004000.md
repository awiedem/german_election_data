## Notes

- WBZ count: 43 Urnen-Wahlbezirke + 12 Briefwahlbezirke for BTW25 (per wahlen-sh.de Stimmbezirk numbering 04000001-04000043 and 04900001-04900012). The user's "~50 expected" matches the 43+12 split; recommend storing Urne and Briefwahl polygons separately if obtained.
- Existing FOI denial (2023, Aktenzeichen via fragdenstaat.de/anfrage/geodaten-der-wahlbezirke-und-wahlkreise-der-stadt-neumuenster/) is the strongest signal that Neumuenster does not maintain WBZ as GIS layers — they are administered descriptively (street-list "Strassenverzeichnis Wahlbezirke"). A renewed FOI is unlikely to succeed without a different framing (e.g. ask for the digital street-to-WBZ assignment table + Stadt's official street polygons, then construct geometries via dasymetric mapping on building/street data).
- Recommended outreach order: (1) wahlen@neumuenster.de cc stadt@neumuenster.de, ask explicitly for the "Strassenverzeichnis je Wahlbezirk" for BTW13/17/21/25 (Excel/CSV is sufficient — geometries can be derived); (2) if a digital layer exists internally in FD 11 (IT) or FD 61 (Stadtplanung), they would be the holders — flag the 2023 FragDenStaat response and ask whether status has changed since 2023.
- Statistik Nord could be approached as a tertiary route: they produced WBZ shapes for Kiel, Luebeck and Flensburg for BTW25 — ask whether Neumuenster was offered/declined and whether they would coordinate a 2025 layer.
- opendata.schleswig-holstein.de and neumuenster.de were both behind Anubis/bot blocks during this audit; FragDenStaat record and Google snippets are the load-bearing evidence. Confidence in "no public layer exists" is high.
