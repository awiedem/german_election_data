## Notes

- 2025 layer has 169 features (verified via WFS RESULTTYPE=hits) — consistent with the expected ~140-170 Urnenwahlbezirke for a Stadtkreis of Freiburg's size (~230k inhabitants, 28 Stadtbezirke / 42+ Stadtteile). NOT statistical districts (those are 184) and NOT Stadtbezirke (28).
- WFS endpoint `abi_wahl` only retains the most recent election cycles (2024 EP/Kommunal, 2025 BTW, 2026 LTW/OBW). Historical 2013/2017/2021 BTW Wahlbezirke are NOT publicly served. The GDI Freiburg CSW catalog (geodaten.freiburg.de/geonetwork) does not list them either.
- A 2021 FragDenStaat request to LWL BW confirmed boundary delineation is municipal: must request directly from the Stadt for historical years.
- Recommended outreach order: (1) gdi-support@freiburg.de (most likely to hold archival shapefiles in internal GIS), (2) wahlamt@freiburg.de (authoritative source for WBZ definitions per election), (3) statistik@freiburg.de (FR.ITZ publishes the OpenData layers — they coordinated the 2025 release).
- Boundaries between BTW 2021 and 2025 in Freiburg are likely very similar; if archival shapefiles are unavailable, the 2025 layer may be a reasonable proxy for 2021 (worth confirming with Wahlamt).
- 2013/2017 likely require manual reconstruction from Wahlamt's internal GIS or scanned Wahlbezirkseinteilung documents.
