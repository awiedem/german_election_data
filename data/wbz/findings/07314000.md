## Notes

- Ludwigshafen does not operate an open-data portal (no opendata.ludwigshafen.de, no ArcGIS Hub, no CKAN). Only public dataset stream is `geodaten.ludwigshafen.de` WMS basemaps (Stadtkarte, Luftbilder, Stadtteilgrenzen, POIs) — no Wahlbezirke layer found in the WMS catalog.
- govdata.de search returned zero Ludwigshafen WBZ datasets. geoportal.rlp.de catalog (519 maps, 25,302 layers) does not list a Ludwigshafen WBZ service — RLP state geoportal hosts ALKIS/BORIS/Basis-DLM but not municipal polling districts.
- City has 10 Ortsbezirke (statistical local districts) — these are the easily mappable units; WBZ polygons (~110 expected, ~144 Stimmbezirke per 2025 announcement which includes Briefwahl) are not separately published.
- Recommended request path: (1) email web form at the Geoinformation und Vermessung department (Bereich 4-16, Rathausplatz 17 / Bismarckstr. 25) requesting WBZ shapefiles for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025; (2) cc Statistikstelle (volker.spangenberger@ludwigshafen.de) — they typically maintain Wahlbezirk geometries jointly with Vermessung; (3) cc Wahlamt (briefwahl@ludwigshafen.de) as election authority.
- No FragDenStaat IFG request found for this AGS; this is a viable fallback if direct request stalls.
- Confidence: high that no public WBZ download exists; moderate-to-high that the city internally holds WBZ geometries (Stimmbezirk announcements reference Wahllokal-level cartography).
