## Notes

- Speyer has ~30 BTW Wahlbezirke (Urne) plus Briefwahlbezirke. A press article (RHEINPFALZ) and city listings reference "59 polling locations" — that figure includes both Urne and Brief, not 59 Urnenwahlbezirke.
- No municipal open-data portal: Speyer participates in RLP statewide geodata distribution (LVermGeo) only for cadastre/topo products. Municipal-thematic layers (Wahlbezirke, Stimmbezirke) are not published.
- Recommended contact path: Vermessung first (vermessung@stadt-speyer.de) — they hold any internal GIS layers used to draw Wahlbezirke; Wahlamt second (wahlen@stadt-speyer.de) for the official Wahlbezirk → street/Stimmbezirk assignment used in BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025; cc bauaufsicht@stadt-speyer.de if Vermessung does not respond.
- Coordinate system expectation if released: ETRS89/UTM32 (EPSG:25832 or 4647) per RLP standard.
- Pre-2013 vintages: no expectation that Speyer retains digital polygon files; archival shapes likely in PDF/paper only.
