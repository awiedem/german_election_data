## Notes

- Stadt Landshut has no functioning open-data portal (opendata.landshut.de does not resolve / no DNS) and no Landshut publisher catalog on open.bydata.de. This contrasts with Ingolstadt and Munich which both publish at least Stadtbezirk shapefiles. Landshut's open-data footprint is essentially zero.
- Election results for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 are published via the AKDB OK.VOTE template (okvote.osrz-akdb.de mirrored at landshut.de/wahlen/...) — this template provides CSV result tables and per-Stimmbezirk HTML pages but does not include any polygon geometry. The same template is used by hundreds of Bavarian municipalities and never exposes shapefiles.
- Stimmbezirk IDs follow the pattern 09261000XXXX (visible IDs span at least 0001..0057 for 2025; 0001..0050+ for 2021; both Urnenwahl + Briefwahlbezirke are present). The ~50 WBZ estimate is consistent with a kreisfreie Stadt of ~73k inhabitants.
- The "Geoinformation & Vermessung" page on landshut.de is a stub navigation node with no published email or phone — atypical compared to peer cities. The only documented inbox is the central poststelle@landshut.de. FragDenStaat lists 9 prior FOI requests to Stadt Landshut (none for WBZ shapefiles), confirming the IFG route via poststelle@landshut.de works.
- ADBV Landshut (poststelle@va-la.bayern.de) is the state surveying authority — it does NOT hold municipal Wahlbezirk geometries; included only for completeness.
- Recommended outreach: (1) poststelle@landshut.de with subject line directed at "Amt fuer Geoinformation & Vermessung (Referat 5)" requesting WBZ shapefiles for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025; cc Wahlamt (Hauptamt, Referat 1) in same mail. (2) If no response, file IFG request via FragDenStaat (template available at fragdenstaat.de/behoerde/20608/stadt-landshut/). (3) Phone fallback: Rathaus 0871 88-0, ask switchboard to forward to Geoinformation & Vermessung.
