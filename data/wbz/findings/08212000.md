## Notes

- Karlsruhe's Wahlkreis 271 (Karlsruhe-Stadt) is divided into 156 Urnenwahlbezirke + 116 Briefwahlbezirke (per city sources for BTW 2025); the 2017 results CSV lists 179 polling-station WBZ. The published GeoJSONs (47 features for 2013; 31 for LTW 2021) are clearly coarse aggregates, not Urnen-WBZ polygons.
- The actual WBZ shapefile DOES exist internally — in July 2017 Karlsruhe responded to a FragDenStaat request and supplied `Wahlbezirke_2017.shp` (227.7 KB) plus `Wahlllokale_BTW2017`. Same path is recommended for 2013/2021/2025: contact Wahlamt and/or Liegenschaftsamt directly, or file an IFG/LIFG request via FragDenStaat.
- The Transparenzportal explicitly invites data requests via the "Datenanfrage" link and `transparenz@karlsruhe.de` — that's the simplest first contact for the missing 2021/2025 vintages.
- Geoportal Karlsruhe (geoportal.karlsruhe.de) and the Urban Data Platform (urbandataplatform-karlsruhe.hub.arcgis.com) host city geodata via WMS/WFS but do not appear to expose Wahlbezirk layers in public search results.
- BW state portals (statistik-bw.de, daten-bw.de) only carry Wahlkreis-level (38 federal constituencies) geometries, not municipal Wahlbezirke — confirmed by the BW Statistical Office: "the division of electoral districts is the responsibility of individual municipalities."
- License for the existing partial files is CC0; assume the full WBZ shapefile would be released under the same terms if requested.
- `wahlamt@karlsruhe.de` is inferred from the standard institutional pattern (the city's official Wahlamt page does not print an email and routes through phone/115); confirm by checking the city's Impressum on the Wahlen page or via the OpenData team.
