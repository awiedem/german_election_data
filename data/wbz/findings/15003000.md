## Notes

- Magdeburg is divided into 148 allgemeine Wahlbezirke + 60 Briefwahlbezirke for the 2026 LTW; 137 districts were used for EU/Kommunalwahl 2024 and BTW 2025. This matches the expected ~150 WBZ count, ruling out confusion with the 9 Stadtteile / ~40 Ortsteile.
- Open data catalogue for Politik & Wahlen lists exactly one item: a static PDF map of the 2024 Wahlbezirkseinteilung. No shapefile, geojson, GeoPackage, WMS, or WFS endpoint was found for any BTW year.
- LVermGeo Sachsen-Anhalt (Otto-von-Guericke-Str. 15, poststelle.lvermgeo@sachsen-anhalt.de) publishes ALKIS/DVG via MetaVer but has no Wahlbezirk layer — these are municipal artefacts, not state cadastre.
- govdata.de has no Magdeburg Wahlbezirk dataset (the "Wahlen Stimmbezirke Wahlbezirke Landtagswahlkreise" hit is Bielefeld, not Magdeburg).
- The "opendata.unser-magdeburg.de" subdomain returned ECONNREFUSED; appears defunct or non-public.
- Recommended next step: email Stadtvermessung@magdeburg.de (CC wahlamt@magdeburg.de) requesting BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 Urnenwahlbezirk geometries — Stadtvermessung will hold the GIS layer used to produce the published PDF map; Wahlamt holds the historical 2013/2017 boundary definitions.
