## Notes

- Schweinfurt is a kreisfreie Stadt (~54k pop). Verzeichnis der Wahlbezirke published as PDF (https://www.schweinfurt.de/media/.../42376_verzeichnis_der_wahlbezirke.pdf, 55 KB) — names/numbers only, no geometry. Could not parse PDF text via WebFetch (binary streams) — recommend manual download to confirm WBZ count.
- ~40 expected WBZ is plausible for a city of this size; OK.VOTE viewer for BTW 2021 (id=ebene_6) lists Stimmbezirke but exact count not extracted via fetch (JS-rendered).
- No municipal open data portal: opendata.schweinfurt.de does NOT exist (DNS/connection refused). Schweinfurt is not currently a publisher on open.bydata.de or govdata.de for election geodata (only LfStaD population aggregates surface).
- Geoportal Bayern / open.bydata.de federated search: 0 hits for "Schweinfurt Wahlbezirk". LDBV Bayern OpenData (geodaten.bayern.de/opengeodata/) provides ALKIS/ATKIS/Verwaltungsgrenzen state-wide but not municipal WBZ.
- Landkreis Schweinfurt geoportal (vianovis.net/lkr-schweinfurt) is for the surrounding rural Landkreis (AGS 09678), not the kreisfreie Stadt — irrelevant here.
- Stadtvermessung sits in Stadtentwicklungsamt (Referat IV), but page explicitly notes "Spatial plans are available exclusively from the Office for Digitalization, Broadband and Surveying" — internal GIS likely exists but no public download. The Stadtvermessung email is the generic Stadtentwicklungsamt inbox; no dedicated geodaten@ address found.
- No Open-Data team / coordinator identified for Schweinfurt — city has no smart-city/open-data program comparable to Würzburg's. Wahlstatistik inbox is the closest functional equivalent for data requests.
- Recommended outreach: primary `stadtentwicklungsamt@schweinfurt.de` (Stadtvermessung — most likely owner of WBZ geometry in internal GIS), CC `wahlen-statistik@schweinfurt.de` (defines the Stimmbezirk attribute table). Use `wahlen@schweinfurt.de` only if statistics inbox doesn't respond. Do NOT contact ADBV — wrong agency for Wahlbezirke.
- BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 boundaries: unknown stability; request all four cycles explicitly. Note that Kommunalwahl 2026 prep (visible on Schweinfurt site) implies up-to-date WBZ definitions exist now.
