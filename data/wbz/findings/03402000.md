## Notes

- Emden is a kreisfreie Stadt (~49k inhabitants, 37 BTW WBZ in 2021 — close to the ~30 expected).
- No municipal open-data portal: opendata.emden.de does not resolve. The Stadt Emden website (FD 210) explicitly references INSPIRE/Nds. GDI obligations but publishes no spatial layers.
- Geoportal Emden (geoportal-emden.de) is a thin Mapbender install with two apps only (heat map, B-Plaene). No Wahlbezirke layer.
- BTW results for Emden are in votemanager (https://votemanager.kdo.de/03402000/) but tabular only — no geometries.

GDI as basis. No need to email LGLN — they hold cadastre, not municipal Wahlbezirke. info@emden.de only as fallback if no reply.
