## Notes

- Cottbus does not appear to publish flat shapefile/GeoJSON downloads for WBZ; the only access path is the cardo3 OGC services (WFS/WMS) at `cardo.cottbus.de/net3/public/ogc.ashx?NodeId=<pgid>`. A shapefile can be reconstructed via `ogr2ogr` from the WFS endpoint (NodeId 1301 for BTW2017, NodeId 1571 for BTW2021).
- Feature counts (86 → 53) confirm these are Urnenwahlbezirke, not the 24 Stadtteile or 19 Bezirke. Drop 2021 to 53 is plausible after the 2019 consolidation reform also visible in pgid=1394 (combined Kommunal-/Landtagswahl WBZ).
- BTW 2013 and BTW 2025: no separate cardo entry. For 2013, ask Statistikstelle (data steward) — they likely retain shapefiles/GIS exports internally even if never published. For 2025, layer may simply not yet have been promoted to the public portal; ask both Statistikstelle and FB 62 Geoportal team.
- License is not explicitly printed on the cardo metadata pages; do NOT assume CC0/dl-de without written confirmation. Include a license question in the outreach email.
- Recommended outreach order: (1) geoportal@cottbus.de — they run the WMS/WFS infrastructure and can grant a flat-file export; cc (2) statisitikstelle@cottbus.de — listed dataset steward who knows BTW2013/2025 boundary versions actually used by the Wahlamt; (3) vermessungsamt@cottbus.de as fallback if no response.
- BB state geoportal (geoportal.brandenburg.de / LGB) was reviewed: provides ALKIS / cadastre / municipal boundaries only — no WBZ-level polygons. govdata.de search did not surface a Cottbus WBZ entry distinct from the cardo records above.
