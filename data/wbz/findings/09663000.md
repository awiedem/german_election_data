## Notes

- Would-be WBZ count: ~165 Stimmbezirke based on numbering observed in 2019 EUW HTML5 reports (e.g. WBZ 165/Lengfeld). Full count for BTW likely 80–170 (in-person + Briefwahl mix); definitely NOT the 13 Stadtbezirke.
- Searched: opendata.wuerzburg.de catalogue API (queries: `wahl`, `stimmbezirk`, `wahlbezirk`, `bundestagswahl`, plus `features=geo`). 33 geo-feature datasets total — only `stadtbezirke` (13 records) is electoral-relevant; all others are non-election geo (Schulen, Mobilfunk, Fahrradzählung, Klimabäume, Adressverzeichnis, etc.).
- The `bundestagswahlen` dataset on the portal aggregates results by `stadtbezirk` only (13 districts), not by Stimmbezirk — so even the tabular WBZ-level results are not on the portal for BTW. WBZ-level results exist only in the HTML5 viewers under `wuerzburg.de/wahlen/Wahl-YYYY-MM-DD/09663000/html5/`.
- The OB-Wahl 2020 dataset (`wahl-des-oberburgermeisters-15320201`) DOES carry per-Stimmbezirk tabular results with `gebiet_nr`/`gebiet_name` — but no geometry attached. This proves the Wahlamt has the Stimmbezirk attribute table; the geometries presumably exist internally but are not published.
- govdata.de CKAN search for Würzburg+stimmbezirk: 0 hits. open.bydata.de: no Würzburg-specific WBZ dataset surfaced via free-text search (federated portal mirrors opendata.wuerzburg.de). Geoportal Bayern (geodaten.bayern.de) carries only state-level products (ALKIS, ATKIS, Verwaltungsgrenzen) — no municipal Wahlbezirke.
- Geostadtplan Würzburg (https://www.wuerzburg.de/themen/bauen-planen/tiefbau-verkehrswesen/geo/geostadtplan/) is a WebGIS viewer maintained by Geodaten und Vermessung — it may render WBZ as a layer internally but does not expose downloadable shapefiles for the public.
- ADBV Würzburg (Amt für Digitalisierung, Breitband und Vermessung) is the state cadastre office, not municipal — they hold ALKIS but not Stimmbezirke.
- Recommended outreach: primary contact `geodaten@stadt.wuerzburg.de` (likely owns/can produce WBZ shapefiles for all 4 elections from internal GIS); CC `wahlen@stadt.wuerzburg.de` (data steward for Wahlbezirk definitions). Open-data coordinator `sabine.foster-jackson@stadt.wuerzburg.de` as tertiary if no response in 1–2 weeks.
- BTW 2013/2017/2021 boundaries likely changed across cycles (Würzburg redistricted Stimmbezirke at least once between 2017 and 2020 based on visible numbering shifts) — request all four cycles separately, do not assume one cuts.
