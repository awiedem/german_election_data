## Notes

- Remscheid is the rare NRW city that publishes WBZ/SBZ polygons as a downloadable open-data layer with explicit dl-de/zero-2.0 (no attribution required). The WFS endpoint serves shapezip directly via `outputformat=shapezip`; both `Wahlbezirke` and `Stimmbezirke` TypeNames are advertised by the WMS GetCapabilities at https://geoportal.remscheid.de/mapserver/wms/wahlbezirkseinteilung?SERVICE=WMS&REQUEST=GetCapabilities&VERSION=1.1.1.
- Remscheid uses "Wahlbezirk" terminology for federal/European elections and "Stimmbezirk" for state/local (per wahlhelfer.remscheid.de wahllexikon). On the WFS, the two layers represent different aggregation levels: ~54 fine-grained polling districts (Stimmbezirke / Urnenwahlbezirke) aggregated into 26 larger Wahlbezirke. For BTW (Urnenwahlbezirk-level) analysis, **the `Stimmbezirke` TypeName (~54 polygons) is the correct match — not the 26 Wahlbezirke**, which are the broader aggregation. Neither layer is the 4-Stadtbezirk level (Alt-Remscheid, Lennep, Lüttringhausen, Süd).
- Single live snapshot only: the published geometry reflects the current (post-26-Feb-2020) boundary plan, valid for BTW 2021 and BTW 2025. For BTW 2013 and BTW 2017 the boundaries were materially different (the Feb-2020 redistricting was triggered by VerfGH NRW 35/19, decided 20 Dec 2019). Historical snapshots are not exposed online.
- Dataset metadata last touched 4 May 2026 (CKAN harvest); content valid since 26 Feb 2020. Open.NRW also lists companion datasets `Remscheider Stadtbezirke` (4 polygons) and `Remscheider Stadtteile` — useful as sanity checks but **not** the WBZ target.
- Recommended next step: fetch current WFS layer once for 2021/2025 (single shapezip download, no email needed). Email marc.manns@remscheid.de (cc geoportal@remscheid.de, wahlen@remscheid.de) requesting archived Stimmbezirk-Stand for BTW 2013-09-22 and BTW 2017-09-24 as Shape/GPKG; mention dl-de/zero-2.0 on the live layer and ask whether the historical snapshots can be released under the same license. Wahlamt likely retains the pre-2020 Wahlbezirkseinteilung in their working files.
- KDVZ NRW / regioit (`wahlen.regioit.de/3/.../05120000`) hosts only tabular WBZ results CSVs for Remscheid BTW years, not polygon geometries.
