## Notes

- WFS endpoint `SG_WFS2/Service.svc` with TypeName `Stimmbezirk1` is documented by a third-party mapping write-up (Jan Degener, Medium 2021, "Stimmbezirke kartieren in NRW"). It returned ~current Solingen Stimmbezirke (city-stated as ~110 polygons), i.e. WBZ-level not 5 Stadtbezirke.
- Direct curl/WebFetch to `geoportal.solingen.de` and `solingen.de/inhalt/...` is blocked by an F5/WAF ("Request Rejected") with both default and browser UA. Neither GetCapabilities nor the Dienste-und-Open-Data page could be programmatically inspected from this environment. A request from a normal browser (or after whitelisting) is required to enumerate FeatureTypes and confirm whether dated/archival Wahlbezirk layers exist.
- No Solingen entries found on govdata.de or open.nrw for Wahlbezirk/Stimmbezirk shapefiles (searched both portals; only Cologne, Düsseldorf, Bielefeld surface for NRW WBZ geodata).
- KDVZ NRW (`wahlen.kdvz.nrw`) hosts only tabular WBZ results CSVs for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 in Solingen, not polygon geometries.
- Recommended next step: email vermessung@solingen.de requesting historical Urnenwahlbezirk-Stand für BTW 2013-09-22, 2017-09-24, 2021-09-26, 2025-02-23 as Shape/GPKG; cc wahlen@solingen.de for date-of-record validation. Mention Nutzungsbedingungen and ask whether re-publication under dl-de/by-2-0 is permitted.
- Solingen's Stimmbezirk numbering pattern (3-digit codes like 111, 121, 261, 433, 512) is consistent across BTW 2013/2017 KDVZ pages, suggesting a stable numbering scheme that should make historical WFS snapshots (if held) directly joinable to results CSVs.
