## Notes

- opendata.jena.de "Politik, Wahlen" group has exactly 2 datasets: Wahllokale (points, GeoJSON+CSV; populated only around active elections) and Ortsteile (41 polygons — NOT the ~70 WBZ).
- Kartenportal Jena (https://kartenportal.jena.de) — public WMS at http://map.jena.de/wms/kartenportal — could not confirm a WBZ layer from search; worth probing GetCapabilities directly.
- govdata.de search returns no Jena-specific Wahlbezirk/Stimmbezirk dataset (only Bebauungspläne).
- Geoportal Thüringen (geoportal-th.de / geomis.geoportal-th.de) hosts statewide layers (ALKIS, Landtagswahlkreise) and metadata for Jena's Ortsteile, but no municipal WBZ polygons.
- Wahlstatistik portals (statistiken.jena.de/wahlstatistik2021_bt.html, .../wahlstatistik2025_bt.html) publish results per Stimmbezirk back to 2002 — implies stable internal numbering, but boundary geometry is not on the public site.
- BTW 2021 Jena had ~71 Urnenwahlbezirke (typical for a city of ~110k); 2025 expected similar count — consistent with target of ~70 polygons, not 41 Ortsteile.
- Best path: email Dr. Meinhardt (Geoinformation) cc Bettenhäuser (Wahlamt) requesting WBZ shapefile for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025; both sit in same Stadtverwaltung and Geoinformation already curates the open-data portal.
