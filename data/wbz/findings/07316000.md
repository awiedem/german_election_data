## Notes

- Neustadt a.d.W. (~53k inhabitants) operates Neustadt Maps as its citizen geoportal but does not publish Wahlbezirke as a layer or download. No opendata.neustadt.eu or CKAN/ArcGIS Hub presence.
- govdata.de search returned zero Neustadt a.d.W. WBZ datasets. geoportal.rlp.de catalog (state-level) carries ALKIS/Bodenrichtwerte/Geobasis but no municipal Wahlbezirke for Neustadt.
- The state-level Vermessungs- und Katasteramt Rheinpfalz (vermka-rpf@vermkv.rlp.de, Tel 06341 149-0, Exterstr. 4, 67433 Neustadt a.d.W.) is NOT the right contact: it is a state cadastre office (sharing the city's name as its dienstort), not the city's election geometry custodian. Skip.
- Recommended request path: (1) primary email Buerger-GIS via stadtmarketing@neustadt.eu (the only published GIS-facing inbox; runs Neustadt Maps and likely holds Wahlbezirk geometries); (2) cc Stadtplanung (stadtplanung@neustadt.eu) - typical co-custodian; (3) cc Wahlamt (wahlen@neustadt.eu) as election authority and data owner of WBZ definitions.
- With ~37 Stimmbezirke total (the requested ~40 Urnen-WBZ matches city scale), historical 2013/2017/2021 boundaries should be reconstructible from city archives if maintained as a GIS layer (the Wahlamt necessarily produces Wahllokal cartography each cycle).
