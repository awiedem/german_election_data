## Notes

- All available WBZ shapefiles are hosted by Amt für Statistik Berlin-Brandenburg under the RBS_OD naming scheme; daten.berlin.de just federates the metadata.
- Naming convention: `RBS_OD_UWB_*` = Urnenwahlbezirke (in-person polling districts only); `RBS_OD_Wahlgebiete_*` (used in 2017) bundles them with related election geometries.
- 2021 file is joint BTW + AGH (same WBZ definition); 2025 is BTW-only.
- 2013 is the gap: searched daten.berlin.de Bundestagswahl tag, AfS opendata index, and FIS-Broker — no public 2013 WBZ geometry surfaced. Worth requesting from Landeswahlamt or AfS directly. A 2013 LWL Berlin shapefile with 1,709 polling-district polygons is referenced in third-party sources but not publicly hosted.
- CRS not stated on dataset pages; expected ETRS89 / UTM Zone 33N (EPSG:25833) per Berlin geodata convention — confirm in `.prj` after download.
- Companion datasets exist for `Wahllokale` (polling-station points) for 2021 and 2025 if needed.
- ODIS also runs https://wahlbezirke.odis-berlin.de/ — likely a visualization frontend rather than a primary download source.
