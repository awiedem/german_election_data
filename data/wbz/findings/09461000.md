## Notes

- Bamberg (~77k inhabitants, ~60k eligible voters) typically has ~45-55 Urnenwahlbezirke for federal elections, matching the "~50 expected" estimate. Briefwahlbezirke are separate.
- Bamberg's BTW results pages live under `stadt.bamberg.de/Bürgerservice/Ämter/Ordnungsamt/Wahlen/Frühere-Wahlen/` and provide only PDF/HTML reports per Stimmbezirk. No GeoJSON/Shapefile/WMS/WFS endpoint for WBZ polygons was found.
- bamberg.bydata.de mirrors the city's open-data publications to the Bavarian federal portal (bydata.de) — Bamberg is a Smart-City model municipality (BMI funded since 2020) but its catalog is small and contains no Wahl-/Stimmbezirk layer. Bayern's federated open.bydata.de search confirms only Munich publishes WBZ shapefiles among Bavarian Kreisfreie Städte.
- Geoportal Bayern (geoportal.bayern.de) and Bayerisches Landesamt für Statistik publish only Wahlkreis (constituency) and Stimmkreis polygons, not municipal WBZ.
- The state surveying office "Amt für Digitalisierung, Breitband und Vermessung Bamberg" (poststelle@adbv-ba.bayern.de, Schranne 3, Tel. 0951 9533-0) is NOT relevant — it handles Liegenschaftskataster for Bamberg/Forchheim/Schlüsselfeld districts, not municipal election geometries. Do not contact.
- A 2021 FragDenStaat IFG request to Baden-Württemberg's Statistisches Landesamt was rejected with "no WBZ geodata held; contact individual municipalities" — confirms the municipal-level path is the only viable one for Bamberg too.
