## Notes

- Oberhausen has ~143 Wahlbezirke (per wahlhelfende.oberhausen.de "Wahllexikon"); also organized into 3 Stadtbezirke / 29 Gemeindewahlbezirke / "Stimmabgabe-, Urnen- und Briefwahlbezirke". The 143 figure is the WBZ target.
- One Open Data Portal Ruhr search hit cited "248 polling districts" (likely older or includes Briefwahl pseudo-bezirke). Either way, the polygon geometry layer is not published.
- Geoportal frontend at geoportal.oberhausen.de redirects to ASmobile login; layer catalog and WFS endpoints not visible to anonymous users. No INSPIRE/OGC service for Wahlbezirke is advertised on www.oberhausen.de/geoportal.
- No Oberhausen WBZ entries appear on GEOportal.NRW, open.nrw, or daten.geoportal.ruhr (Regionalverband Ruhr) for any of the four BTW years.
- Recommended path: write to bereich.vermessung_und_kataster@oberhausen.de (geo primary) and CC wahlen@oberhausen.de + joerg.juelkenbeck@oberhausen.de, requesting Urnenwahlbezirk polygons (shp/gpkg) for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025, citing dl-de/by-2.0 as preferred license. Statistik (Jülkenbeck) likely holds the working WBZ geometry used to generate Wahlatlas.
- Wahlatlas at oberhausen.de/.../statistik/wahlatlas.php is rendered (DUVA-based) but underlying polygons are not exposed for download.
