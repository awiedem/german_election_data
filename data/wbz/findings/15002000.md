## Notes

- WBZ polygon shapefiles for Halle are NOT openly published anywhere I could locate (KomGIS+, halgis.halle.de, metaver.de, geodatenportal-sachsen-anhalt, govdata.de all negative for "Wahlbezirk" + Halle). Polygons exist internally — wahlergebnisse.halle.de renders choropleth maps per Stimmbezirk for BTW 2021 and BTW 2025 — but are not exposed for download.
- Feature-count sanity check: BTW 2025 has 126 Urnen-Wahlbezirke + 60 Briefwahlbezirke (per official Stadt comms cited in search results), so ~150 polygons is the expected WBZ-level scale, well above the 5 Stadtbezirke / 35 Stadtteile aggregations. Polygons rendered on wahlergebnisse.halle.de match this scale.
- LVermGeo LSA (Landesamt fuer Vermessung und Geoinformation Sachsen-Anhalt; poststelle.halle.lvermgeo@sachsen-anhalt.de) is NOT the right contact: their geobasis credit on Halle's election maps applies to surrounding Saalekreis frames, not WBZ definitions. WBZ definitions are owned by the municipality.
- Recommended outreach: primary stadtvermessung@halle.de (likely custodian of the GIS layer), cc statistik@halle.de / wahlamt@halle.de (data steward of WBZ definitions), and opendata@halle.de (to request publication on KomGIS+). Ask explicitly for BTW 2013, 2017, 2021, 2025 WBZ as Shape/GeoPackage/GeoJSON; expect 2013/2017 to require archive retrieval since boundaries change between BTW.
- No FragDenStaat request specifically targeting Halle WBZ was found (the closest hit, request #46, targeted Stadt Ladenburg).
