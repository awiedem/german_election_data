## Notes

- Baden-Baden runs ArcGIS Server at geoportal.baden-baden.de (services: Flaechennutzungsplan, Gebaeude, Luftbilder, Schulwege, Ueberflutung). REST root inspected — no Wahl/Wahlbezirk/Statistik service exists.
- gis.baden-baden.de (Disy Cadenza viewer) was unreachable (ECONNREFUSED) at time of search; layer list not separately enumerable, but Disy is a viewer over the same backend, so absence at REST root implies absence here too.
- daten-bw.de query for Stadt Baden-Baden as publisher returns Mietspiegel, Haushaltsplan, and OB-Wahl 2022 results (CSV) — no spatial Wahlbezirk dataset. Compare to Freiburg/Karlsruhe/Heidelberg/Stuttgart, which all publish WBZ shapefiles on daten-bw or own portals.
- City has 39 allgemeine Wahlbezirke (per news/Wahlbekanntmachung context) — close to your ~40 estimate; geometry must be requested from the city.
- Recommended request path: email vermessung.geoinformation@baden-baden.de (CC stefan.hetzel@baden-baden.de) requesting Urnenwahlbezirk-Polygone for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 as Shapefile or GeoPackage; CC wahlen@baden-baden.de for cross-reference of WBZ-IDs to election results. Mention DL-DE-BY-2.0 / CC-BY-4.0 as acceptable license.
- govdata.de not separately searched for Baden-Baden WBZ — daten-bw.de federates BW datasets and showed nothing, so independent govdata coverage is highly unlikely.
- LGL Open-Data Portal (opengeodata.lgl-bw.de) provides only Geobasisdaten (ATKIS, ALKIS, DGM, Wahlkreise), not municipal WBZ.
