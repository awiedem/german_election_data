## Notes

- Landau operates a city GeoPortal (geoportal.landau.de + maps.landau.de, MapServer-based) with an explicit "Wahlen" thematic application showing Wahllokale + Stimmbezirke. However, the WMS layer is **viewer-only** — no Shapefile/GeoJSON/WFS download is exposed publicly, GetCapabilities returns 403 to scripted clients, and there is no open-data portal CKAN/ArcGIS Hub feed.
- City confirms 27 Urnenstimmbezirke "unabhängig von Kommunal-, Landtags- oder Bundestagswahl", suggesting boundaries are stable across election types and likely across the 2013/2017/2021/2025 BTW (no announced redistricting found). This means a single current-vintage Shapefile from Vermessung is likely sufficient as a proxy for all four years — but historical snapshots should still be requested to confirm (boundaries can drift even if count is constant).
