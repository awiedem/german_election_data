## Notes

- Stimmbezirke (192 polygons) are the polling-district unit relevant for Bundestagswahlen. The prompt's "~300 WBZ" estimate is high - portal and Open.NRW metadata both confirm 192. Wuppertal also publishes 33 "Kommunalwahlbezirke" (party-list districts for municipal council, larger units) - separate dataset, not relevant for BTW.
- Single dataset URL is overwritten across vintages; only the most recent boundary set is publicly archived. Historical 2013/2017 geometries are not on the portal.
- Files exist in EPSG:4326 (WGS84) and EPSG:25832 (ETRS89/UTM32) for both shp and geojson, plus KML. Bundles include a PDF with usage terms.
- For BTW-specific results, the city's election portal at https://wahlen.wuppertal.de/bw2025/05124000/ publishes only CSV (results, polling locations, streets) - no separate Wahlbezirk geometry; BTW results map to the same 192 Stimmbezirke.
- For 2013/2017 archival shapes, contact geodatenzentrum@stadt.wuppertal.de with a written request specifying the election dates.
