## Notes

- WFS GetCapabilities confirmed: service title "Stimm- und Wahlbezirke", provider "Stadt Herne / FB 52/4", layers `stimmbezirke` (82 features) and `wahlbezirke` (27 features), both Polygon, EPSG:25832/4326/3857. Bounding box 7.122–7.298°E, 51.503–51.574°N matches Herne extent.
- Sanity check on counts: Herne has 4 Stadtbezirke and 13 Stadtteile; 82 features clearly is at WBZ/Stimmbezirk granularity. 27 `wahlbezirke` features = Kommunalwahl-Wahlbezirke (council single-member districts), NOT what we want for BTW.
- Metadata stated publication 2020-02-17, last updated 2024-05-13, maintenance "asNeeded" — the layer is current snapshot only, no archived historical versions exposed.
- Reference to PDF "Wahl- und Stimmbezirke ab 2020" on the Wahlen page strongly implies a redistricting occurred in 2020; therefore BTW 2013 and BTW 2017 used different (smaller or differently numbered) Stimmbezirke that are not published online.
- Recommended outreach: email kataster-geoinformation@herne.de + wahlen@herne.de jointly requesting Stimmbezirk shapefiles valid for BTW 2013-09-22 and BTW 2017-09-24. CC opendata@herne.de.
- Direct download example (no auth needed): `curl "https://geodaten.herne.de/geoserver/wahlen/bezirke?SERVICE=WFS&VERSION=1.1.0&REQUEST=GetFeature&TYPENAME=stimmbezirke&OUTPUTFORMAT=shp" -o herne_stimmbezirke.zip`
- License DL-DE Zero 2.0 confirmed both via opendata.ruhr metadata and inline WFS `<ows:AccessConstraints>`.
