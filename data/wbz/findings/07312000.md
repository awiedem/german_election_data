## Notes

- Kaiserslautern operates a real OpenData portal AND a Geoportal — unusual quality for an RP city. Both BTW and KW WBZ layers are downloadable as GeoJSON under CC0, no registration required.
- Verified BTW file: 326 KB, 76 features, single property `Wahlbezirk` (4-digit string codes like "0250", "0210"), WGS84 (EPSG:4326). Polygon count 76 is consistent with Urnen-WBZ scale; rules out Stadtbezirke (4) and Ortsbezirke (31). KW Stimmbezirke file is 101 features (finer split for municipal elections, larger than BTW count because Kommunalwahl uses smaller districts).
- Stand 17.02.2022 means the geometry corresponds to the boundaries used for BTW 2021 (held 26 Sep 2021); same boundaries were carried into Landtagswahl 2021 and Stand 2022 indexing. For BTW 2013 / 2017, the city would need to provide archived snapshots — these almost certainly exist internally in the Statistik und Wahlen GIS but are not in the open-data portal.
- For BTW 2025: portal not updated post-2024-07-18 with a 2025 vintage; press references cite 60 WBZ for recent elections, which conflicts with the 76 in the 2022 file — boundaries may have been consolidated. Confirm with statistik@kaiserslautern.de.
- Recommended request path: (1) email statistik@kaiserslautern.de (data owner) requesting BTW 2013 / 2017 / 2025 WBZ GeoJSON snapshots and confirmation that Stand 2022 = BTW 2021 boundaries; (2) cc opendata@kaiserslautern.de (publisher) and wahlen@kaiserslautern.de (Wahlamt). The state Vermessungs- und Katasteramt Westpfalz (LVermGeo RLP) does NOT hold municipal WBZ — do not route there.
- No FragDenStaat IFG request found for AGS 07312000; viable fallback if response stalls. Note FragDenStaat has a generic 2025 WBZ campaign aimed at all municipalities.
- Confidence: high that BTW 2021 Stand-2022 layer is the published WBZ; high that 2013/2017 archives exist internally; moderate on whether 2025 WBZ count differs from the 2022 file.
