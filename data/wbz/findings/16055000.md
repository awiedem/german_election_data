## Notes

- No opendata.weimar.de portal exists. stadt.weimar.de "Offene Geodaten" page (Schwanseestr. 17, same building as Wahlbüro) lists only raster products from the state GDI-Th (DTK10/25/50/100, ÜKTh250N, GTh250) — no Wahlbezirk vector layer.
- govdata.de federated search returns no Weimar Stimmbezirk/Wahlbezirk dataset (only other cities: Düsseldorf, Bonn, Hamburg/SH, etc.).
- Geoportal Thüringen (geoportal-th.de) hosts statewide ALKIS, Landtagswahlkreise, and Bundestagswahlkreise — no municipal WBZ layer for Weimar.
- Cadastral matters in Weimar are split: Stadt Weimar runs its own city GIS (Bodenmanagement), while the Thüringer Landesamt (poststelle.apolda@tlvermgeo.thueringen.de, Apolda branch) handles regional ALKIS — but neither maintains election WBZ.
- BTW 2025 in Weimar: Stimmbezirke numbered at least up to 52 (e.g. WBZ 52 "THIS II" – Thuringia International School; WBZ 29 Weimar-West). Stadt Weimar (~65k pop) → ~40-55 Urnenwahlbezirke matches the user's ~40 estimate.
- Wahlbüro and Bodenmanagement share the same building (Schwanseestr. 17) — a single email cc'ing both has the highest yield. Bodenmanagement holds the GIS; Wahlbüro authoritatively defines WBZ assignments per election.
- Federal Bundestagswahlkreis 192 (Erfurt – Weimar – Weimarer Land II) shapefiles are available via Bundeswahlleiterin/BKG but at constituency level, not WBZ — out of scope.
