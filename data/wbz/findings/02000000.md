## Notes

- Shape and GeoJSON ZIPs for 2017, 2021, 2025 are direct, hot-linked, no-auth downloads on statistik-nord.de under predictable paths `/fileadmin/Dokumente/Wahlen/Hamburg/Bundestagswahlen/<year>/Vor_der_Wahl/`. 2025 also has a `_geojson.zip` sibling; 2017 and 2021 only ship as shapefile (one zip each).
- Feature counts marked ~1300 are taken from the Statistikamt/Transparenzportal description ("ca. 1.300 kleinraeumige Einheiten"); not independently verified by opening the files.
- 2013: searched FragDenStaat, statistik-nord.de archive, transparenz.hamburg.de, and the Geoportal — only tabular Erststimmen/Zweitstimmen results surface. Recommend a direct ask to LGV (poststelle@gv.hamburg.de) and Statistik Nord (info@statistik-nord.de) for an archived WBZ-2013 shape; LGV maintains historical Wahlbezirk geometries in its DB.
- A live WFS / WMS for "Geodaten zu Wahlen von Hamburg" is registered in MetaVer (UUIDs 64403BC7-... and CE156F6B-...) and in GovData; it serves the current election's WBZ rather than a versioned archive, so it is useful for 2025 but not for backfilling 2013-2021.
- Hamburg is a Stadtstaat: the seven Bezirke and ~104 Stadtteile are separate datasets and should not be confused with the ~1300 Wahlbezirke.
