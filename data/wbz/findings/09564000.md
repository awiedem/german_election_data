## Notes

- Stadt Nürnberg deliberately consolidates: same Stimm-/Wahlbezirk geometry covers KW, LTW, BTW, EW; only labelling differs. So the 2025 polygons can be used for BTW 2025 directly.
- For BTW 2013/2017/2021 polygons, the only documented path is the explicit request route: "Geometrien zu den Nürnberger Stimm- und Wahlbezirken können Sie unter Angabe des Verwendungszweckes über unser Kontaktformular anfordern." Form: https://www.nuernberg.de/global/ajax_kontaktformular.html?cfid=79532
- Boundary stability: city had 363 Urnen-WBZ for BTW 2025; prior cycles had similar order of magnitude but exact counts and cuts differed (re-zoning periodically). Treat 2025 polygons as proxy only for 2025.
- Datenwahlen portal (datenwahlen.nuernberg.de/bt2021/...) provides per-WBZ result CSVs back to BTW 2005, but no geometries are bundled there.
- No city-level open-data portal at opendata.nuernberg.de; "OpenData" pages live under the Statistik subsite. govdata.de search for AGS 09564000 yields no WBZ shapefile entry as of May 2026.
- No public WFS/WMS endpoint advertised for Stimm-/Wahlbezirke on GeoPortal Nürnberg.
- No emails surfaced in scrape; both relevant Ämter route external contact through web forms. Phone numbers are the only direct channel: Statistik 0911/231-2840, Geoinformation 0911/231-4401.
