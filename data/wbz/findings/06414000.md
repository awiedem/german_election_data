## Notes

- Wiesbaden runs ~200 Urnenwahlbezirke for federal elections. Tabular WBZ-level results from 2001 onward are available via the Wahlatlas (https://wahlergebnisse.wiesbaden.de/) and Excel exports, but the WBZ polygon layer is not currently published on either the Open Data portal or the Geoportal.
- The new Open Data platform (opendata.cloud.wiesbaden.de) launched Feb 2026; built jointly by Amt für Statistik und Stadtforschung, Smart City, and Innovation/IT. Wahlbezirk geometries could plausibly be added on request — primary lever is geodaten@wiesbaden.de (Stadtvermessung holds the boundary file used by the Wahlamt).
- Geoportal Wiesbaden (geoportal.wiesbaden.de) and the public WFS at geodata.o-sp.de only expose urban-planning layers (Bebauungsplaene, Flaechennutzung, Satzungen, Veraenderungssperren, LU.SpatialPlan) — no Wahl layer.
- Hessisches Statistisches Landesamt does not hold municipal WBZ geometries; municipalities are responsible (confirmed in earlier searches).
- Recommended ask: request Urnenwahlbezirk shapefile/GeoPackage as used for BTW 2013, 2017, 2021, 2025 from geodaten@wiesbaden.de, CC wahlen@wiesbaden.de and amt-fuer-statistik-und-stadtforschung@wiesbaden.de. Note Wiesbaden does not have re-cuts every cycle, so the same boundary set may cover multiple BTWs.
