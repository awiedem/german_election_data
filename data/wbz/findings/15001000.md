## Notes

- Dessau-Roßlau has 25 Stadtbezirke and 57 statistische Bezirke that are explicitly described by the Stadt as identical to the Wahlbezirke. Expected ~57 polygons for Urnenwahlbezirke — somewhat below the user's "~50" hint and well below typical big-city scales; matches the city's own documentation.
- govdata.de search returned zero Dessau hits for Wahlbezirk/Stimmbezirk; MetaVer has only a generic "Geoportal" and "Geodienste" record for Dessau-Roßlau without any Wahlbezirk layer entry; LVermGeo Sachsen-Anhalt holds geobasis/cadastre, not Wahlbezirke.
- geoportal.dessau-rosslau.de (WebOffice) advertises themes such as Stolpersteine, Wanderwege, Postleitzahlen, Verwaltungsgrenzen, Baulückenkataster, Baumkataster — no Wahlbezirk theme is referenced in the public catalogue. TLS certificate-verification error on a direct fetch attempt prevented full enumeration; recommend manual browser check.
- No FragDenStaat request specifically targeting Dessau-Roßlau Wahlbezirke was located.
- Recommended outreach: primary geodienste@dessau-rosslau.de (custodian of GIS layers under Frau Lievertz / Frau Tannert), cc statistik@dessau-rosslau.de and wahlen@dessau-rosslau.de (data stewards of WBZ definitions, including 2013/2017 historical boundaries). Request BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 Urnenwahlbezirke as Shape/GeoPackage/GeoJSON; expect 2013/2017 to require archive retrieval since boundaries shift between BTW. Propose publication on the city geoportal under DL-DE-BY-2.0 to formalise.
