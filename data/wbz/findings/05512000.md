## Notes

- ArcGIS REST layer `MapServer/3` (Stimmbezirke) has exactly 69 polygon features — matches expected ~70 WBZ (NOT the 6 Stadtbezirke at layer 1 nor the 12 Kommunalwahlbezirke at layer 2). Layer 0 = Wahlräume (point polling locations, also 69).
- No `wahljahr`/`gueltig_von` attribute — service is a snapshot of the current configuration. Historical 2013/2017 cuts are not exposed online; archived shapefiles (if retained) must be requested from Wahlamt or Amt 12.
- opendata.ruhr / GovData / open.NRW expose only `Stadtbezirke Bottrop` (6 polygons, upper level) and `Kommunalwahlbezirke Bottrop` (~12, municipal council districts) — NEITHER is the WBZ layer needed.
- KRZN Wahlarchiv (https://wahlarchiv.krzn.de/) provides tabular Stimmbezirk results for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 but no geometries.
- Bottrop is NOT in the opendata.ruhr `wahlraume-bundestagswahl-2025` dataset (that one is Dortmund-only).
- Recommended ask: contact Wahlamt (wahlen@bottrop.de, attn. Pascal Gniffke) cc gis@bottrop.de for archived Stimmbezirke geometries for BTW 2013, 2017, 2021, 2025 — they own the layer per geoportal.ruhr metadata; SHP package is marked usage-on-request.
