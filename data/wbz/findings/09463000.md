## Notes

- Coburg has NO open-data portal (no opendata.coburg.de exists) and no Coburg-specific entries on open.bydata.de or govdata.de for WBZ. The "OpenData CSV-Dateien" hits returned by search are Landkreis Coburg (AGS 09473xxx) result pages from votemanager-style election software — tabular results only, no polygons, and not for Stadt Coburg (kreisfreie Stadt 09463000).
- Municipal Wahlbezirkseinteilung confirmed: 40 in-person Stimmbezirke + 35 Briefwahlbezirke for BTW 2025 (source: coburg.de/mein-coburg/artikel/2026/februar/wahlbenachrichtigungen-unterwegs.php). Matches the ~25-WBZ expectation order of magnitude (slightly higher; ~41k Wahlberechtigte ÷ ~1k per Bezirk).
- Geoportal page (coburg.de/.../geo-informationsdienste) advertises a metadata catalog but does not expose downloadable layers; the city's "Geoinformationsdienste" service page (Mandy Linz contact) lists Stadtplan/Bebauungsplaene/Orthophotos/Liegenschaft etc. as fee-based products and does NOT list Wahlbezirk among published layers, suggesting the polygons exist internally (Wahlamt holds the Einteilung; Vermessung holds the geometry) but have never been a published product.
- Landkreis Coburg's Geoportal at vianovis.net/lkr-coburg/ covers the surrounding rural municipalities (AGS 09473xxx), not the kreisfreie Stadt Coburg.
- ADBV Coburg (adbv-coburg.de, Amt fuer Digitalisierung, Breitband und Vermessung) handles state-level Liegenschaftskataster only — NOT relevant for municipal WBZ. Same pattern as other Bavarian cities (Ingolstadt, Erlangen, etc.).
- Recommended outreach: (1) Katja.Hagmayer@coburg.de cc Mandy.Linz@coburg.de requesting WBZ polygon shapefiles for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 (all four boundary versions if changed); (2) if no response, einwohneramt@coburg.de (Wahlamt) which owns the authoritative Wahlbezirkseinteilung and can authorize the Vermessungsabteilung to release the geometries. Note: Coburg's geodata service page lists fee schedule (15.50 EUR per A4 paper plan up to 62 EUR per A1 PDF, "Datensatzkosten je nach Umfang") so a small fee may apply.
