## Notes

- Only the **current** WBZ layer is publicly distributed (file dated 2024-05-07; matches the partition used for BTW 2025). 55 features, properties limited to `nummer`, `bezeichnung`, `guid`. Higher than the ~40 user expectation — plausibly inflated by Brief-/Sonderwahlbezirke counted as separate features; verify with attribute table once loaded.
- License is **dl-de/by-2-0** — the citizen-facing download page does not print this, but the WFS GetCapabilities `AccessConstraints` and the GovData record confirm it. Required attribution string: `© GeoDaten-KVA / Stadt Frankfurt (Oder), dl-de/by-2-0`.
- **Historical BTW geometries (2013, 2017, 2021) are not online.** The WFS service replaces the layer in place; no time-versioned archive. Request directly from Wahlbüro (data steward, owns the boundary cutbacks per election) cc Kataster- und Vermessungsamt (likely keeps GIS snapshots — they are the publisher named in the dl-de attribution).
- BB state geoportal (geoportal.brandenburg.de / LGB) does not provide WBZ-level polygons — confirmed via Cottbus/Potsdam reviews and explicit BB statement that polling-district geometry is municipal responsibility.
- Recommended outreach: single email to Wahlbuero@frankfurt-oder.de cc katasteramt@frankfurt-oder.de requesting "Wahlbezirks-Geometrien (Shape/GeoJSON) der Bundestagswahlen 2013, 2017, 2021" — explicitly cite the existing dl-de/by-2-0 publication of the current layer to make the precedent clear.
