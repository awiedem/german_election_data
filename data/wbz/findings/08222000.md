## Notes

- Open-Data catalog (mannheim.opendatasoft.com) hosts only tabular historical election results (Oberbürgermeisterwahl 1948-2015, Gemeinderatswahlen 1926-2014, Landtagswahlen 1925-2011) — no Wahlbezirk polygon datasets surfaced under "wahl" or "wahlbezirk" search. No BTW results datasets at all.
- Geoportal Mannheim (geoportal-mannheim.de) lists a "Kommunalstatistik" service with small-scale spatial divisions including Wahlbezirke as a category, but service catalog is gated/not enumerated in the public OGC index; WFS standards 1.0/1.1/2.0 supported in principle.
- WahlatlasMannheim (https://web2.mannheim.de/wahlatlas/) — InstantAtlas viewer, allows download of result tables by Stadtteil/Stimmgebäude/Wahlbezirk but no geometry export.
- Mannheim has ~280 Urnenwahlbezirke per the user's brief; nothing observed contradicts this. No publicly downloadable polygon file confirmed for any of the four target BTW years.
- Recommended outreach: primary statistikstelle@mannheim.de (data owner) and wahlbuero@mannheim.de (election operations); cc opendata@mannheim.de to request publication on the Open-Data portal; vermessungsamt@mannheim.de for technical/CRS/format follow-up.
