## Notes

- Leipzig's open-data portal publishes WBZ geometries only for the two most recent BTW (2021 and 2025). No 2013 or 2017 shapefiles are listed on the Bundestagswahl tag page or the Amt fuer Statistik und Wahlen org page (21 BTW datasets total, none for those years).
- 2021 file confirms ~405 Urnen-Wahlbezirke, matching the expected ~400+ scale. 2025 file feature count not stated on portal but should be similar.
- License field is empty on both shapefile resource pages; safe to ask Amt fuer Statistik und Wahlen to confirm DL-DE-BY-2.0 / CC-BY explicitly.
- For 2013/2017, the institutional path is statistik-wahlen@leipzig.de (data steward of WBZ definitions) with cc to geoinformation@leipzig.de if internal GIS layer needed. Older WBZ shapefiles likely exist in the Amt's GIS archive but were never uploaded to the open-data portal.
- Briefwahlbezirke (mail-in) are published as a separate dataset for 2025 only; 2021 and earlier appear to be Urnen-only.
