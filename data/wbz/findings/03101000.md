## Notes

- Only one current edition of the WBZ shapefile is published; no year-stamped historical layers (BTW2013/2017) appear anywhere on opendata.braunschweig.de, govdata.de, the NI geoportal record (d6ab9af3-d1f3-44ad-908d-8ff703250fe2), or in Google searches. Historical boundaries must be requested from Geoinformation directly.
- ZIP labeled "wahlbezirke.zip" was downloaded and inspected: 6-file shapefile bundle, 175 polygons, EPSG:25832, attribute carries WBZ number. This matches expected ~170 Urnenwahlbezirke (NOT the 19 Stadtbezirke, which are a separate dataset at /dataset/stadtbezirke).
- Internal file name `SP_Wahlbezirke2021_area.shp` and metadata "Stand: Mai 2021" indicate the served file is still the 2021 edition despite the portal showing a 2025-01-17 "last updated" stamp — the update may be metadata-only. Worth a one-line email to opengeodata@braunschweig.de to confirm whether a refreshed BTW2025 boundary file exists and to request 2013/2017 archives.
- Wahlberichte archive (https://m.braunschweig.de/.../Wahlberichte.html) is tabular results only — no geometries.
- Recommended outreach order: opengeodata@braunschweig.de (primary, holds the file), cc wahlen@braunschweig.de (knows the BTW boundary versions used), LGLN as fallback only if city does not respond.
