## Notes

- Stadt Hof has no public open-data portal (`opendata.hof.de` does not exist; nothing on open.bydata.de / govdata.de matches Hof WBZ). The only city geodata channel is the read-only Geoportal (geoportal.hof.de) showing Stadtplan, Flaechennutzungsplan, and Bebauungsplaene — no Wahl-/Stimmbezirk layer.
- The "Kommunale Karten und Geodaten; Erhalt" service page confirms geodata is supplied on request (paid/by arrangement) via the Stadtvermessung unit; formats and fees not published online.
- Landkreis Hof Geoportal (geoportal.landkreis-hof.de) is being migrated to a "Digital Twin" system and currently redirects users to BayernAtlas; it covers Landkreis municipalities, not the kreisfreie Stadt Hof (AGS 09464000).
- Election results for Stadt Hof at WBZ level live on `wahl-hof.de` (HTML/PDF only). The 2020 Stadtratswahl page uses 12-digit Stimmbezirk IDs `094640000001`... confirming the city's internal WBZ coding but no geometry export.
- ~30 WBZ is plausible for a kreisfreie Stadt of ~46k inhabitants; Frankenpost reporting on Hofer Land Stimmbezirke supports this order of magnitude. Briefwahlbezirke are likely separate.
- State open-data layers (geodaten.bayern.de ALKIS Verwaltungsgebiete) provide only Gemeinde/Landkreis polygons, not sub-municipal WBZ.
- Recommended outreach: (1) lageplan@stadt-hof.de cc stadtplanung@stadt-hof.de requesting Wahlbezirks-/Stimmbezirks-Shapefiles for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 (Geo-Unit holds the polygons); (2) if no response within 2 weeks, wahlen@stadt-hof.de (Wahlamt) which owns the Wahlbezirkseinteilung; (3) post@stadt-hof.de as last-resort general inbox.
