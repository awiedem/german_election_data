## Notes

- Stadt Ansbach is a kreisfreie Stadt in Mittelfranken (Bavaria); not to be confused with the surrounding Landkreis Ansbach (AGS 09571).
- The city runs its own self-hosted votemanager-clone results pages (wahlen.ansbach.de). Unlike the regio-iT/KDO-hosted votemanager mirrors, this build does NOT expose an /daten/opendata/ subtree. CSV result tables may be scraped per-Stimmbezirk page but no geometries are bundled.
- Stimmbezirk count is in the right ballpark for the brief (~25): 34 (BTW 2021), 31 (BTW 2025) Urnen-Stimmbezirke. Counts dropped between 2021 and 2025 (likely consolidation of small bezirke).
- govdata.de search for "Ansbach Wahlbezirk" yields zero hits (verified May 2026). open.bydata.de returns only Bevoelkerung dataset for AGS 09561, no Wahlbezirk geodata.
- okfde/wahldaten quellen_wahlbezirke.csv lists Ansbach as votemanager-platform but with the Landkreis page URL - confirming no city-level WBZ shapefile feed is registered there either.
- BayernAtlas / GeoPortal Bayern host LDBV cadastre layers but do not carry city Stimm-/Wahlbezirk polygons; that responsibility rests with the city Wahlamt.
- All four BTW vintages (2013, 2017, 2021, 2025) require an explicit data request to wahlen@ansbach.de. Recommend asking specifically for Shape/GeoJSON of Urnen-Stimmbezirke for each Wahltag, plus the underlying Strassenabschnittsverzeichnis (street-section register) in case polygons are not maintained.
- Bayern statewide elections (LTW 2013, 2018, 2023) reuse the same Stimmbezirk-Einteilung as the contemporaneous BTW in most Bavarian cities, so a single set of historical polygons would likely cover both election families per cycle.
