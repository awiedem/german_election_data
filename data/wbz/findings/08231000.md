## Notes

- No public WBZ shapefile exists for Pforzheim across any of the searched portals (opendata.pforzheim.de does NOT exist; pforzheim.de/opendata returns no dedicated open-data hub; daten-bw.de Stadt Pforzheim publisher exposes only 11 non-spatial CSVs; govdata.de search returns zero hits for "Pforzheim Wahlbezirk"; Geoportal BW catalog likewise empty).
- BürgerGIS (https://buergergis.pforzheim.de/, ArcGIS-based, run by Vermessungs- und Liegenschaftsamt with infrastructure from Amt für Digitalisierung und Organisation) is the most plausible internal holder of WBZ polygons but no Wahl-themed app is publicly listed in its app catalog (categories: Bürger, Stadt, Kultur, Freizeit & Tourismus).
- Pforzheim is a Stadtkreis (BW): roughly 80 Urnenwahlbezirke expected for BTW (sanity bound vs. 5 Stadtteile / 16 Stadtbezirke). Not verifiable without data in hand.
- Recommended request path: email plv@stadt-pforzheim.de AND BuergerGIS@pforzheim.de in parallel, asking explicitly for Urnenwahlbezirk polygon shapefile/GPKG for BTW 2013, 2017, 2021, 2025 (specify: not Stadtteile, not Stadtbezirke, not Wahlkreis 279). CC wahlen@pforzheim.de so the Wahlamt can authorize release of historical WBZ-Schnitte.
- BW state-level fallback confirmed dead: Statistisches Landesamt BW and Landeswahlleitung explicitly state they hold no WBZ geodata — boundary-setting is the responsibility of each of BW's 1,101 municipalities.
- BTW 2013/2017 archival snapshots particularly unlikely to be retained outside the Vermessungs- und Liegenschaftsamt; ask for any internal version-controlled WBZ feature class.
