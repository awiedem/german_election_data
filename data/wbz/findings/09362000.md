## Notes

- The Abteilung Statistik (statistik@regensburg.de) is the data steward: it draws Wahlbezirk boundaries jointly with the Wahlamt, sells the geometries as zipped shapefiles or EMF (Bessel 1841 / GK Zone 4; ETRS89/UTM available for +5 EUR), and ships only the full city in one delivery — not subsets.
- Free downloads on the Statistik geodata page exist only for Stadtgrenze, Stadtbezirke (the 18 large districts), Gewässer, Alleengürtel, Autobahn, Eisenbahn — Wahl-/Stimmbezirke are explicitly NOT in the free set.
- Stadt Regensburg has no general open-data portal of its own; the Geoportal (https://geoportal.regensburg.de) is a Masterportal viewer with cadastre/cycling/climate themes and no election layer. open.bydata.de hosts only Landkreis Regensburg datasets, not Stadt Regensburg WBZ.
- Wahlbericht PDFs (https://www.regensburg.de/buergerservice/statistik/publikationen/wahlberichte) document each BTW; result-table URLs use 6-digit Stimmbezirk IDs (e.g. ...000121 for BTW 2025), indicating ~120 in-person WBZ — consistent with WBZ (not the 18 Stadtbezirke or 37 Unterbezirke).
- Recommended outreach: single email to statistik@regensburg.de copying wahl@regensburg.de, asking whether (a) historical BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 WBZ shapefiles can be supplied free of charge for academic use under a documented license, or (b) the standard 70 EUR / 40 EUR fee per election year applies. vermessung.geoinformation@regensburg.de is unlikely to hold WBZ geometries (they handle cadastre/topography).
- FragDenStaat IFG request is a fallback if the paid quote is rejected; Bayerisches Informationsfreiheitsgesetz coverage of municipal statistics offices is partial.
