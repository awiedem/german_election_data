## Notes

- Searched suhl.eu (Bürgerservice, Ordnungs-/Bürgeramt, Dezernate-und-Aemter, politik-wahlen), Google for "Suhl Wahlbezirk shapefile", "Suhl Stimmbezirk geodaten", govdata.de federated search hits, and the Thüringen geoportal (geoportal-th.de). No municipal-level WBZ geometry is published.
- Cadastral surveying for Suhl is handled by the state (Thüringer Landesamt für Bodenmanagement und Geoinformation, Außenstelle Schmalkalden, poststelle.schmalkalden@tlvermgeo.thueringen.de). They handle ALKIS cadastre but not election WBZ boundaries.
- Suhl is a small kreisfreie Stadt (~37k pop) with no dedicated GIS or open-data unit; election administration sits inside the Ordnungs- und Bürgeramt. A direct request via poststelle@stadtsuhl.de or ordnungsamt@stadtsuhl.de is the only realistic path.
- Federal Bundestagswahlkreis 196 (Suhl – Schmalkalden-Meiningen – Hildburghausen – Sonneberg) shapefiles exist via Bundeswahlleiterin/BKG, but those are at Wahlkreis (constituency) level, not WBZ — out of scope.
