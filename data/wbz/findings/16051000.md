## Notes

- Erfurt geoportal lists only 4 Verwaltung/Planung datasets (Verwaltung POIs, Bewohnerparkgebiet, Fernwärmesatzung, Stadtteile). No election-related layer published as open data.
- govdata.de search "Erfurt Wahl" returns 0 hits. Thüringen geoportal (geoportal-th.de) hosts only Landtagswahlkreise statewide, not municipal WBZ.
- Wahllokalfinder (erfurt.de) suggests internal GIS exists; geometry must be requested from Wahlamt (statistik@erfurt.de) or Geoportal team (geoportal@erfurt.de).
- Statistics+Wahlen and Geodaten/Geoportal both sit under "Amt für Informationstechnik und Statistik" — same parent unit, single contact path likely sufficient.
- Vermessungs-/Katasteramt for Erfurt is state-level (TLBG, kataster@tlbg.thueringen.de) — not the right contact for municipal WBZ.
- Historical WBZ (2013/2017/2021) almost certainly not maintained as a versioned shapefile series; expect at best a current/most-recent layer plus archived PDF maps.
