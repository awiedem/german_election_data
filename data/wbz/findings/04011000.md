## Notes

- Bremen city-state: AGS 04011000 = Stadt Bremen only. Bremerhaven (AGS 04012000) is separately served via https://www.bremerhaven.de/.../buerger-und-ordnungsamt-statistik-und-wahlen.35583.html (CC BY-NC-ND, BTW 2021 shapefile).
- INSPIRE download endpoint serves "Urnenwahlbezirke" (in-person polling districts) — exactly the target geometry, not Stadtbezirke (5) or Ortsteile (88).
- ZIP fetched (257 KB) lists components `WBZ_2020.{cpg,dbf,prj,sbn,sbx,shp}` — confirms it is a polygon shapefile of WBZ. Feature count not opened here; expected ~440 for Stadt Bremen.
- 2013/2017 vintages: not visible on open portals. Bremen's WBZ are stable across federal elections in many years, so 2020 layer may be a usable approximation; for exact historical boundaries, IFG/email request to wahlamt@statistik.bremen.de is the correct path. A precedent IFG response exists at transparenz.bremen.de (Antrag 165458).
- 2025 vintage: MetaVer "letzte Aktualisierung 28.04.2026" suggests a refresh, but the archive's filenames still read 2020. Verify with Wahlamt before using for BTW 2025.
