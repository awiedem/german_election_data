## Notes

- Rostock is a model open-data city: WBZ shapefiles for all four target BTW years (2013, 2017, 2021, 2025) are openly published under CC0 with no scraping/contact required.
- All four files live under predictable URL stem `https://geo.sv.rostock.de/download/opendata/bundestagswahl_<year>/bundestagswahl_<year>_wahlbezirke.{shp.zip,json,kml,gml,csv,xlsx}`.
- File sizes (~160-190 KiB SHP) are consistent with WBZ polygon counts in the ~120-150 range — well above the 5 Ortsamtsbereiche or 31 Stadtteile alternatives. Stadtbereiche/Ortsteile are separate datasets at opendata-hro.de/dataset/stadtbereiche and /ortsteile.
- 2013 dataset description explicitly says "Wahlbezirke und Briefwahlbezirke" — may include Briefwahl polygons (zero-area or coincident with WBZ) that need filtering.
- OpenData.HRO portal is operated by KVLA (the cadastre/surveying office) — geo and open-data contacts overlap.
- City Wahlamt direct email not surfaced in searches; contact via Hauptamt or rathaus.rostock.de service portal if data clarification needed.
- No action needed for Landkreis Rostock — this AGS (13003000) is the kreisfreie Hansestadt only.
