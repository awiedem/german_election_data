## Notes

- WFS GetCapabilities exposes two relevant FeatureTypes: `Stimmbezirke` (137 polygons — matches expected ~120 WBZ scale) and `Kommunalwahlbezirke` (29 polygons — these are NOT Bundestag WBZ; these are municipal Kommunalwahl districts). For BTW, use `Stimmbezirke`.
- Bbox confirms full Hamm city (lon 7.67-8.00, lat 51.58-51.75), not just a sub-area; geometries are real polygons (verified by GetFeature on Stimmbezirke.1 returning a multi-vertex polygon).
- 7 Stadtbezirke and Stadtteile would be a different (and smaller-feature-count) dataset; 137 features rules that out.
- The dataset is a single live snapshot (current Stimmbezirke). No multi-year archive published. Wahl-Atlas (https://www.hamm.de/rathaus-politik/wahlen/wahl-atlas) explicitly states pre-2017 results were recalculated to 2017 boundaries, implying historical WBZ geometries are stored internally but not distributed.
- For 2013/2017/2021 BTW geometries, an email request to wahlen@stadt.hamm.de (CC yannik.polkowski@stadt.hamm.de) is the recommended path.
- Catalog metadata records: GDI-DE record 3ab882c0-0311-45e6-9691-398e8a5621ee ("Wahlbezirke der Stadt Hamm") and Hamm open data record `kommunalwahlbezirke-2025-in-der-stadt-hamm` reference the same WFS endpoint.
- Usage limitation on GDI-DE record reads "verwaltungsintern freigegeben" while the opendata.ruhr record assigns DL-DE-BY-2.0 — the open data portal listing should govern external use.
