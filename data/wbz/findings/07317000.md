## Notes

- No municipal open-data portal exists. Domains opendata.pirmasens.de and geoportal.pirmasens(.de) do not resolve. govdata.de search for Pirmasens returns ~105 hits (mostly Verwaltungsgliederungspläne PDFs and Pirmasens-Land documents, not city WBZ).
- geoportal.rlp.de / lvermgeo.rlp.de hosts state-level ALKIS/Verwaltungsgrenzen but no municipal Wahlbezirke for Pirmasens. State catalog contains no Pirmasens-specific layer (only Trier among RLP cities is listed with city-level services).
- Pirmasens uses CAIGOS GIS (kommunales Stadtplanung-Tool) internally — high confidence WBZ geometries exist there but are not published. No web-facing CAIGOS viewer found at pirmasens.de.
- BTW 2025 city announcement: 7 Stadtbezirke divided into 28 Urnenwahlbezirke + 17 Briefwahlbezirke; ~30,440 eligible voters. WBZ layout is published only as Wahllokal address list, not polygons.
- The Vermessungs- und Katasteramt Westpfalz (vermka-wpf@vermkv.rlp.de, Bahnhofstr. 24, 66953 Pirmasens, 06331 5011-1150) is the **state** LVermGeo office and handles ALKIS, not municipal Wahlbezirke — do not contact it for WBZ.
- Recommended request path: (1) email info@pirmasens.de addressed to "Abteilung Stadtplanung — CAIGOS GIS" requesting WBZ-Shapes/GeoPackage for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 in dl-de/by-2-0; (2) cc the Wahlbüro (06331 84-2542) as data owner of election geometries; (3) cc Statistikstelle (same Abteilung) since they typically curate Bevölkerungs-/Wahl-Geometrien jointly. Fallback: FragDenStaat IFG to Stadtverwaltung Pirmasens (no prior request on this topic).
- Confidence: high that no public WBZ download exists for any of the four target years; high that the city internally holds WBZ geometries in CAIGOS for all four elections (2013-2025).
