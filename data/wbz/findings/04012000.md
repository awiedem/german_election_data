## Notes

- Bremerhaven AGS 04012000 is an independent kreisfreie Stadt within Land Bremen, administered by the Magistrat — separate from Stadt Bremen. The state-level Geoportal Bremen / `Wahlbezirke_HB.zip` (CC BY 4.0) covers Stadt Bremen only; it does NOT contain Bremerhaven WBZ. Confirmed via the 04011000.md sibling file.
- WBZ count of 78 (BTW 2025) / 79 (EW 2024) matches the magnitude expected for Bremerhaven (~75 polling locations per the BTW 2021 press release) — these are Urnenwahlbezirke, not the 9 Stadtteile or 2 Stadtbezirke (Nord/Süd).
- Publishing pipeline: shapefiles are produced by the Vermessungs- und Katasteramt (vermamt@) but distributed via the Wahlamt page (statistik@). For historical (BTW 2013/2017/2021) vintages, route the request to **both** addresses — vermamt likely holds the archived datasets, statistik confirms electoral validity.
- License is CC BY-NC-ND 3.0 DE (Creative Commons Namensnennung-NichtKommerziell-KeineBearbeitung 3.0 Deutschland) — note the NC and ND clauses are more restrictive than the CC BY 4.0 used by Stadt Bremen. Required attribution: "© Magistrat Bremerhaven, Vermessungs- und Katasteramt".
- IFG precedent: FragDenStaat request "geodaten-der-wahlbezirke-24" / Transparenzportal Antrag 165458 (March 2021) successfully obtained the BTW 2021 archive within ~25 days at no cost. A follow-up IFG/email request for 2013/2017/2021 historical files is the documented path forward; cite the prior request to expedite.
- 2021 archive verification attempted via Wayback Machine — Internet Archive was 503 / "Temporarily Offline" at audit time (May 2026); retry later to recover the archived `Shape_Bundestagswahl2021_Bremerhaven.zip` if still cached.
