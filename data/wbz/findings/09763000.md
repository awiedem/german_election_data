## Notes

- Kempten has 36 Urnenwahlbezirke for BTW 2025 (per the published CSV) — well below the ~50 expected in the brief. Historic counts likely similar.
- Only a CSV+PDF street→Stimmbezirk crosswalk for BTW 2025 is published; no polygon geometries (shp/geojson/gpkg) for any BTW cycle. Older BTW (2013/2017/2021) have nothing online — not even tabular.
- The Bavarian state-level Vermessungsamt for the area is the ADBV Immenstadt - Außenstelle Kempten (Herrenstr. 8, poststelle-ke@va-immen.bayern.de). That office maintains cadastral/ALKIS data but does NOT hold municipal Wahlbezirk polygons — those are city-internal. Listed here only to disambiguate; do NOT contact for WBZ.
- The geoportal (geoportal.kempten.de) renders Wahlbezirk-style boundaries only as part of the interactive Stadtplan; it does not expose them as a downloadable layer. The Nutzungsbedingungen explicitly state Geodaten are copyrighted with non-commercial use only — i.e., no dl-by-de baseline like München/Augsburg.
- Best path: email gis@kempten.de (primary) requesting BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 Stimmbezirk polygon shapefiles, cc'ing wahlen@kempten.de (sets the boundaries; Stabstelle holds the geometry). Mention reuse of the existing BTW 2025 street-list data and that polygons clearly exist internally.
- No Kempten datasets surface on govdata.de or open.bydata.de search for Wahl/Stimmbezirk; the Bayern federation does not mirror geoportal.kempten.de open-data files.
- opendata.kempten.de does not resolve (ECONNREFUSED); the de-facto open-data endpoint is geoportal.kempten.de/opendata/.
