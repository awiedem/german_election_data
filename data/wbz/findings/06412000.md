## Notes

- Offene Daten portal (`offenedaten.frankfurt.de`) has been migrated to a JS-rendered SPA (Domino InformationPortal); legacy CKAN-style resource URLs surfaced by Google now 404 server-side, but the dataset slugs `wahlatlas-2015-geodaten` and `wahlatlas-2021` are confirmed in current search results. Recommend using a headless browser or contacting `offene.daten@stadt-frankfurt.de` to retrieve the live resource UUIDs.
- Feature-count caveat: the city's 2026 Kommunalwahl uses ~448 WBZ; BTW 2025 reports 376 *general* Urnenwahlbezirke (statistikportal.frankfurt.de). The "~500" expectation in the task brief is too high — Frankfurt's BTW WBZ count is ~370–475 depending on year and Sonderwahlbezirke handling. Verify via shapefile attribute table after download.
- 2013 and 2017 share the same WBZ partition; 2021 may differ slightly; 2025 was recalculated to 2026 territorial status, so it is not directly comparable to the 2015/2021 Wahlatlas geodata without reaggregation.
- `geoinfo.frankfurt.de` redirects to "Offline" (deprecated). The active geoportal is `geoportal.frankfurt.de`, which serves WMS/WFS but did not surface a Wahlbezirk layer in this triage.
- Email `geodaten@stadt-frankfurt.de` is the institutional inbox of the Stadtvermessungsamt's Geo-Service-Center — preferred for shapefile/format requests over individual analyst addresses.
