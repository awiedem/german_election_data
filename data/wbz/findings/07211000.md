## Notes

- City has 59 Urnenwahlbezirke + 59 Briefwahlbezirke for BTW 2025 (per Wahlarchiv page). 59 is the right scale for the Urne polygon set - this is well above the 19 Ortsbezirke (Trier has 19 Stadtteile/Ortsbezirke), so any layer with ~19 features is NOT the WBZ layer.
- No standalone open-data portal for Stadt Trier (no opendata.trier.de). All geodata syndication runs through geoportal.rlp.de and govdata.de under dl-de/by-2-0; GovData search returned no Trier Wahlbezirk dataset.
- Statistikstelle and Bodenmanagement/Geoinformation share the same building (Gerty-Spies-Str. 2/3) - both depts likely involved in Wahlbezirk maintenance. Statistikstelle is institutional custodian of kleinräumige Gliederung incl. Stimmbezirke; Bodenmanagement holds the GIS layer.
