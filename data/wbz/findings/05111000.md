## Notes

- Düsseldorf publishes geometry under a single dataset "Wahlgebietseinteilung Düsseldorf - Stimmbezirkseinteilung" with vintages 2017, 2019, 2020, 2025; only the 2025 vintage adds a BTW-specific Wahlbezirk file (`Btw_Wahlbezirke_2025_*`). Other years: BTW Wahlbezirke must be derived by aggregating Stimmbezirke via the `wahlbezirk`/`wb` attribute.
- A separate dataset "Wahlgebietseinteilung Düsseldorf - Bundestagswahlen" exists but contains only the 2 Bundestagswahlkreise (105/106) polygons — not the ~400 polling-district geometries.
- Pre-2017 (BTW 2013) Stimmbezirk geometry is not on the open-data portal; Wahlamt or Vermessungs- und Katasteramt would need to be contacted directly for archival shapes.
- All files use either ETRS89/UTM32 (EPSG:4647) or WGS84 (EPSG:4326). KML mirrors exist.
- Coverage assumption: Düsseldorf has ~400 Stimmbezirke; per-year feature counts not displayed on portal pages and not fetched here (would need to download the GeoJSON to verify).
