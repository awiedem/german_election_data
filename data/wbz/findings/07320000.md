## Notes

- Zweibrücken does not operate an open-data portal (no opendata.zweibruecken.de). The only city geo-service is geoportal-zweibruecken.de (viewer only — Stadtkarte, B-Plaene, Bauplaetze, Topo/Luftbilder); no Wahlbezirke layer surfaced in catalog.
- Vermessung function: per zweibruecken.de, Vermessung is "bei den Stadtwerken Zweibrueken angesiedelt" (sits at the municipal utility) — not a typical city Vermessungsamt. Liegenschaftskataster is in the Stadtbauamt (bauamt@zweibruecken.de). Best GIS custodian path: Stadtbauamt.
- Best institutional request: cc all three. Primary = Stadtbauamt (bauamt@zweibruecken.de) — operator of the geoportal and likely holder of any digitised WBZ layer. cc Wahlamt (wahlen@zweibruecken.de) — definitional owner of WBZ for each election. cc Stadtplanung (stadtplanung@zweibruecken.de) — backup GIS contact.
- Confidence: high that no public WBZ download exists; moderate that a digital WBZ layer exists internally for 2021/2025 (geoportal infrastructure is in place; consolidation announcement implies cartographic decision-making); low-to-moderate for 2013/2017 (small kreisfreie Stadt with limited GIS staff; pre-2024 WBZ may exist only as Strassenverzeichnis tables).
- ~20 Urnenwahlbezirke expected scale: matches a small kreisfreie Stadt of ~34k inhabitants. City has 8 Stadtteile/Ortsbezirke — any layer of that size is NOT the WBZ layer.
- No FragDenStaat IFG request for AGS 07320000 located (16 visible requests on the authority page cover sirens, ÖPNV, Denkmalschutz, libraries — none on Wahlbezirke). Viable fallback if direct request stalls.
- RLP convention reconfirmed: LVermGeo RLP and geoportal.rlp.de do not host municipal Wahlbezirke; responsibility lies with each kreisfreie Stadt.
