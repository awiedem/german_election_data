## Notes

- No city open-data portal exists (no opendata.worms.de). Worms publishes geodata exclusively through https://geoportal-worms.de/ and a register of OGC WMS endpoints at https://www.worms.de/neu-de/zukunft-gestalten/planen-und-bauen/stadtvermessung/kartographie/OGC_Dienste.php
- The "Wahlen" WMS layer covers Wahllokal point standorte ("Standorte der Wahllokale bei Europa-, Bundes-, Landes- und Kommunalwahlen"), not polygon Wahlbezirke. No WFS endpoint is exposed for Wahlen, and no Wahlbezirke or Stimmbezirke layer is in the OGC service register.
- A "statistische-bezirke" WMS exists (https://geoportal-worms.de/ogc/wms/statistische-bezirke) but those are statistical districts (Stadtteile/Stadtbezirke), not WBZ — Worms has 13 Stadtteile vs. 44 BTW WBZ; sizes do not match.
- Worms is divided into 44 allgemeine Wahlbezirke for Bundestags- and Landtagswahlen (per worms.de/Bundestagswahlen and /Landtagswahlen pages) and 60 for Kommunal-/Europawahlen. The ~50 expected estimate matches the BTW count.
- LVermGeo RLP open-data shop (https://lvermgeo.rlp.de/geodaten-geoshop/open-data) carries only geobasis (ALKIS, ATKIS, DTK, orthos), not municipal Wahlbezirke. Geoportal RLP (https://www.geoportal.rlp.de/) likewise does not redistribute Worms WBZ.
- govdata.de search for Worms WBZ returns nothing (only Bielefeld, Münster, etc.). No FragDenStaat success record visible for Worms.
- Best institutional path: Stadtvermessung 6.2 (Henning Stramm, holds the city's GIS layers) via the city contact form, with cc to Statistikstelle (Michael Born — likely custodian of kleinräumige Gliederung incl. Stimmbezirke/WBZ) and wahlen@worms.de (data owner for election-specific WBZ definitions). Historical WBZ for BTW 2013/2017 likely exist as internal GIS layers given that Worms maintains current WBZ in its Geoportal Stadtplan.
