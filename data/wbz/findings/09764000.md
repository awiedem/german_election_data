## Notes

- Memmingen is a Bavarian kreisfreie Stadt (~45k inhabitants); no dedicated Open-Data portal — datasets flow via open.bydata.de → GovData. Election geometries have never been published.
- For BTW 2025 the city has 25 Urnenwahlbezirke (matches expected ~25). Numerical per-Stimmbezirk results live under https://www.memmingen.de/fileadmin/wahl/ but only as HTML tables (e.g. URLs of form `wahl/2025_BTW/...097640000NNN.html`).
- maps.memmingen.de (vianovis web map) does NOT expose a Wahlbezirk theme; visible layers are 3D-Ansicht, route planning, Kartenstile. No public WMS/WFS endpoint discoverable.
- WBZ polygons almost certainly exist internally — Memmingen's Stadtplanung/GIS unit (Amt 51) maintains the address-to-Stimmbezirk assignment for the Wahlamt. Custodianship: Wahlamt sets boundaries, Stadtplanung holds the geometry.
- Recommended outreach: email stadtplanung@memmingen.de (cc wahlamt@memmingen.de) requesting historic Wahlbezirk polygon shapefiles for BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025 under dl-by-de/2.0 (license they already use for other geodata). Personal contact: Christian Hohner (christian.hohner@memmingen.de) appears to be the GIS lead in Stadtplanung.
- Bavarian state alternative: open.bydata.de has Munich's WBZ as model dataset (https://open.bydata.de/datasets/https-opendata-muenchen-de-dataset-wahlen-stimm-und-briefwahlbezirke-in-muenchen) — Memmingen has no equivalent.
- Bundeswahlleiterin only publishes Wahlkreis (255 Memmingen-Unterallgäu) boundaries, not WBZ.
