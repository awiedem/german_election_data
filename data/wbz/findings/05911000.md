## Notes

- Confirmed Stimmbezirk count for BTW 2017 = 248 polygons (matches the "~250 WBZ" expectation), so these are true WBZ-level polygons, not the 6 Stadtbezirke or ~30 Stadtteile.
- Bochum publishes Stimmbezirk polygons only for BTW 2017, BTW 2025, LTW 2017, EuW 2019, EuW 2024. There is no published BTW 2013 polygon layer and no standalone BTW 2021 layer.
- For BTW 2021, boundaries should equal those used since Kommunalwahl 2020. Best workaround: use the BTW 2025 layer or LTW 2017 / EuW 2019 layer as a proxy after verifying against the published BTW 2021 result tables; or request the 2021 snapshot from geodatenmanagement@bochum.de.
- For BTW 2013, a polygon layer almost certainly never went onto open data. Either (a) reconstruct from the older WMS (see Jan Degener, "Stimmbezirke kartieren in NRW", Medium) or (b) email geodatenmanagement@bochum.de citing prior reconstruction efforts.
- ArcGIS REST endpoint: https://geoservicekkm.bochum.de/arcgis/rest/services/maponline/Wahlen/MapServer — useful for direct WFS-style pulls; the request timed out once during this audit, retry recommended.
- License caveat: 2025 dataset is under a closed license; reuse for academic publication should be cleared with opendata@bochum.de.
