## Notes

- Brandenburg an der Havel does **not** publish WBZ polygons online for any of the four target years. The city's GDI portal has a clean services catalogue and explicit dl-de/by-2-0 license, so an outreach request is well-positioned but indispensable.
- 53 + 13 (Urnen + Brief) WBZ for BTW 2025 inferred from city's election results page; expected ~50 WBZ matches well.
- Recommended outreach: single email to **geodatenservice@stadt-brandenburg.de** cc **statistik@stadt-brandenburg.de**. The Geodatenservice runs GDI.BRB and can produce shapefile exports; the Statistikstelle owns the historical WBZ boundary definitions (incl. pre-2024 reorganization). Ask for shp/GeoJSON for BTW 2013, 2017, 2021, 2025 + license confirmation (dl-de/by-2-0 vs CC BY).
- `katasteramt@stadt-brandenburg.de` rendering on the city site is missing the `@` in the source HTML — likely an obfuscation artifact; verify before use, or rely on `geodatenservice@…` which is unambiguously printed in the GDI contact page.
- Wahlbericht PDFs published per cycle on city site contain WBZ cartographic figures — useful as a fallback for digitizing if no shapefile is obtained.
- Geoportal Brandenburg (LGB / state) provides only administrative boundaries (Verwaltungsgrenzen WMS/WFS) down to municipality level; no sub-municipal WBZ for BRB.
