## Notes

- Bayern is a centralized-Vermessung state: cadastral / ALKIS data is held by the state ADBV, but WBZ/Stimmbezirk geometries are an organisational responsibility of the kreisfreie Stadt's Wahlamt. Passau, like most Bayern kreisfreie Staedte, has not published these polygons as open data.
- The city runs BTW result presentation via votemanager (`wahl.passau.de`), which exposes per-Stimmbezirk result HTML pages and CSV (`opendata-strassen.csv`-style street directory mapping addresses → Stimmbezirk), but not polygon geometries.
- Only confirmed Stadt-Passau open geodata service: B-plan/FNP WMS/WFS at `https://geodata.o-sp.de/service/passau.cgi` (DL-DE-BY-2.0). No Wahl-related layers in this service.
- Stadt Passau is NOT listed as a publisher on open.bydata.de (Bayern open-data portal) for any election-geometry datasets as of the search date (May 2026).
- Recommended ask: email wahlen@passau.de cc poststelle@passau.de requesting "WBZ-/Stimmbezirks-Geometrien (Shapefile/GeoJSON) fuer Bundestagswahlen 2013, 2017, 2021, 2025 — Urnenwahlbezirke, vorzugsweise als jahresgenaue Stand-Datei." The Stadtplanungsamt (stadtplanungsamt@passau.de) likely actually holds the GIS layer; cc them.
- ~31 Stimmbezirke matches the user's "~40 expected" within a plausible range (small kreisfreie Stadt). Briefwahlbezirke are a separate set; ask whether Briefwahl polygons exist or whether Briefwahl is only Stadt-weit.
- WebFetch was blocked by the Passau geoportal ArcGIS REST root (403); a manual browser visit to `https://geoportal.passau.de/ArcGIS/rest/services/` is the next step to confirm whether a non-public WBZ layer exists in another folder (e.g. APPDATEN_WGS84 has a layer 3 referenced in indexed search results).
