## Notes

- Delmenhorst does NOT publish a Wahlbezirk shapefile/GeoJSON. The only city-published geodataset registered in the Niedersachsen Geoportal is "Verwaltungseinheiten Stadt Delmenhorst" (Stadtbezirke/Unterbezirke/Baublöcke) — administrative units, not polling districts.
- KRIS (Kommunales Raumbezogenes InformationsSystem) is the in-house GIS unit and almost certainly holds the WBZ polygons used to print Wahlbenachrichtigungen — they should be the primary point of contact for an archived shapefile request covering BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025.
- WBZ count not directly stated; the 2025 PDF lists Wahlbezirke (~50 expected for a city of ~78k inhabitants is plausible, but could not be confirmed from PDF binary inspection).
- Recommended request: email kris@delmenhorst.de (cc wahlen@delmenhorst.de) asking for archived Urnenwahlbezirk polygon shapefiles per BTW year (2013, 2017, 2021, 2025), preferably as Shapefile/GeoJSON in EPSG:25832, with WBZ ID matching the Wahlbezirksverzeichnis. Mention DL-DE/BY-2.0 as acceptable license.
- No FragDenStaat request for Delmenhorst found (the one search hit was for Baden-Württemberg).
- No Niedersachsen-state-level WBZ shapefile service exists; municipalities are individually responsible.
