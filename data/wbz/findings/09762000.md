## Notes

- Kaufbeuren (~44k inhabitants) ran 25 in-person Stimmbezirke + ~12 Briefwahlbezirke for BTW 2025 (per osrz-akdb result page format). Tabular Stimmbezirk-level results published at wahlen.kaufbeuren.de via osrz-akdb (kommunaler IT-Dienstleister AKDB).
- Geoportal maps.kaufbeuren.de runs on the "touvia" platform (Vianovis, Allgaeu). OSM-based with municipal overlays. Confirmed layer list (kreisbote.de Apr 2024) does NOT include Wahlbezirke. No documented WMS/WFS endpoint.
- The "Vermessungsamt Kaufbeuren" external hits all refer to the state ADBV Marktoberdorf (Kurfuerstenstr. 19, 87616 Marktoberdorf, Tel 08342/7009-0) — handles Liegenschaftskataster, NOT municipal WBZ. Not relevant for WBZ shapefile request.
- Kaufbeuren is a kreisfreie Stadt and thus owns its own Wahlbezirkseinteilung internally. The Wahlamt (Referat 300) maintains the definition; the IKT department (Wittek/Gebert) is the technical operator of the geoportal and likely holds any digital geometry. There is no separate Vermessungsamt within the city administration.
- No dedicated "geo@kaufbeuren.de" or "vermessung@kaufbeuren.de" found; no Open-Data office or beauftragte/r identifiable. Kaufbeuren has no standalone open-data portal; it appears in open.bydata only via aggregated Bayerisches Landesamt fuer Statistik datasets.
- Recommended outreach: (1) wahlen@kaufbeuren.de for WBZ definition + cc info@kaufbeuren.de routing to Abteilung IKT (Hr. Gebert, GIS-Verantwortlicher) for geometry export. Mention all four BTW years (2013/2017/2021/2025) in one request. Phone backup: 08341/437-389 (Wahlamt) or 08341/437-0 (Zentrale).
- Honest assessment: low probability that historic 2013/2017 polygons were ever digitized — small kreisfreie Stadt with limited GIS staffing; current Wahlbezirke likely exist as ArcGIS/touvia internal layer but were not necessarily versioned across elections. Boundaries may have stayed nearly static since 2013, in which case one current polygon set + Strassenverzeichnis lookups suffices.
