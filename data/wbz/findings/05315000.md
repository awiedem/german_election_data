## Notes

- Publisher/owner of the WBZ shapefile is Raumbezogene Statistik (statistik-rbs@), not Vermessungsamt — for this dataset that is the right primary contact.
- Projection: ETRS 1989 UTM Zone 32N (EPSG:25832) for all shapefiles; KMZ/JSON in WGS84.
- Feature counts confirm WBZ granularity (503-800 small polygons) — not the 9 Stadtbezirke or 86 Stadtteile.
- The 503-feature count for the current file reflects Köln's 2024 consolidation (1:1 Urne↔Briefwahl). For BTW 2013/2017 use the 800-feature archives; for BTW 2021 use the 543-feature 11/2021 file; for BTW 2025 use the current `Stimmbezirk_19.zip`.
- 2013 archive lacks .cpg encoding file (older ESRI export); attribute schema documented in `Stimmbezirk_Attribute.csv` for 2021.
- Briefwahlbezirk-to-Stimmbezirk crosswalk (post-2024): https://www.offenedaten-koeln.de/sites/default/files/distribution/Briefwahlbezirk.csv
