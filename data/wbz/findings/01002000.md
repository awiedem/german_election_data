## Notes

- The WFS endpoint `lhkiel:Wahlbezirke` is a single, current-state layer; querying it today returns the 107-polygon 2025 layout. There are NO year-versioned layers for 2013/2017/2021. Sister layers in the same WFS: `Wahllokale`, `Gemeindewahlkreise`, `Landtagswahlkreise`, `Kinder-_und_Jugendwahlbezirke`.
- WFS metadata says "Landeshauptstadt Kiel, Lizenz CC-BY-4.0" (no DL-DE), and the service is listed in the SH OpenData portal under the Kiel publisher page (opendata.schleswig-holstein.de/organization/landeshauptstadt-kiel).
- Pre-reform Kiel had ~150-170 WBZ (consistent with the user's expectation); the 2024/25 reform reduced this to 107 for BTW 2025. Historical 2013/2017/2021 polygons are likely held internally by the Geodatenservice (ESRI shapefile archive behind the ArcGIS Server) — recommended ask: email geodatenservice@kiel.de cc opendata@kiel.de citing the 2022 IZG-SH precedent (FragDenStaat link above) and request "archivierte Wahlbezirks-Geometrien BTW 2013/2017/2021".
- Kiel.de was unreachable from WebFetch (Anubis bot-block) so contact emails were derived from search snippets and the public service-detail pages; institutional inboxes (`geodatenservice@`, `wahlen@`, `opendata@`) are the canonical Kiel patterns and are listed across multiple Kiel.de organisational-unit pages.
- Statistik Nord publishes only interactive ArcGIS web maps for SH BTW 2017/2021/2025 (no downloadable shapes for Kiel WBZ); the Hamburg-style packaged WBZ ZIPs do not have an SH/Kiel sibling.
- Briefwahl: joint Briefwahlbezirke appear to be folded into the WBZ labels (`011/015` etc.) rather than as separate features; verify before treating as Urne-only polygons.
