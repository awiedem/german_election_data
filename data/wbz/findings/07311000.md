## Notes

- Frankenthal does NOT operate an open-data portal. There is no opendata.frankenthal.de, no CKAN/ArcGIS Hub, no published GeoJSON. The city's "Geoinformationen" page only points to geoportal.rlp.de (state portal) for cadastral, Bauleitpläne, Bodenrichtwerte, Luftbilder — no Wahlbezirke layer.
- govdata.de search returned zero Frankenthal WBZ datasets. geoportal.rlp.de does not catalog municipal WBZ for Frankenthal.
- Frankenthal is part of Wahlkreis 206 (Ludwigshafen/Frankenthal). City has ~30 expected Urnen-WBZ; exact count for individual BTWs not published. The Abteilung Geoinformation is small (3 named staff: Bruns, Mannstein, Sema) but is the explicit municipal owner of digital spatial data per the city's own description.
- Recommended request path: (1) email zentraledienste@frankenthal.de subj. "Abteilung Geoinformation - Anfrage Wahlbezirk-Geodaten BTW 2013/2017/2021/2025" addressed to Dr. Benjamin Bruns (Abteilungsleitung); (2) cc briefwahl@frankenthal.de (Wahlamt) as election authority; (3) optionally cc stadtverwaltung@frankenthal.de. The state Vermessungs- und Katasteramt Rheinpfalz (Landau) does NOT hold municipal WBZ — do not route there.
- No FragDenStaat IFG request found for AGS 07311000 (Stadtverwaltung Frankenthal Pfalz behörde page exists but no WBZ-related request). Viable fallback if direct request stalls.
